## I

In the United Kingdom the absence of general banking legislation providing joint stock banks with limited liability and government's recognition as early as the 1830s that 'exchange' banks connecting colonial or other trading partners with the home banking centre —what may be described as 'London and X' banks - incurred unusual risks forced the development of the colonial banking regulations administered under the ultimate authority of the Treasury. Policy was devised to offset the admitted risk by permitting limited liability but in turn countering this 'advantage' by imposing terms and conditions designed to protect both shareholder and 'constituent' or customer. These compromises were confirmed in charters, embodied with Treasury approval either in an act of the colonial legislature or in a Royal Charter. The act or charter carried with it certain potential privileges, including the right to receive local treasury business and to issue banknotes, subject, in the latter case, to any local prohibitions.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">在英国，由于缺乏为合股银行提供有限责任的一般性银行业立法，加之政府早在 19 世纪 30 年代就已认识到，那些连接殖民地或其它贸易伙伴与本土银行中心（可称之为“伦敦与某地”银行）的“汇兑”银行承担着非同寻常的风险，这迫使殖民地银行法规在财政部的最终授权下得以发展。政策设计旨在通过允许有限责任来抵消公认的风险，但反过来又通过施加旨在保护股东和“委托人”或客户的条件与条款来制衡这一“优势”。这些折中方案在特许状中得到确认，并经财政部批准，以殖民地立法机构法案或皇家特许状的形式体现。 该法案或特许状附带若干潜在特权，包括开展地方国库业务及发行钞票的权利，但就发行钞票而言，须遵守当地任何相关禁令。</span></span></span>

Exchange banks might be founded either in the overseas territory or in London, but, except in colonies of settlement, the founding directors eventually returned home, re-establishing their bank by Royal Charter in London. The Hongkong Bank was exceptional; the founding directors indeed returned 'home' but the decision-makers remained in the East, and the Bank's head office remained in Hong Kong. But in other cases the overseas territorial government consequently lost control of 'their' bank and, depending on the extent of their authority, might impose limitations on <span id="Ch05.xhtml_page_86"></span>the return of the bank in its reorganized form. The Indian government and the legislatures of the Canadian and Australian colonies took especial concern to retain control of their banking sectors —and thus the Chartered Bank of India, Australia and China, for example, was restricted in its attempts to establish in Australia.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN" style="">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">汇兑银行可能设立在海外属地或伦敦，但除了殖民定居地外，创办董事最终都会返回母国，凭借皇家特许状在伦敦重建他们的银行。汇丰银行是个例外；创办董事确实返回了"母国"，但决策层留在了东方，银行总部也设在香港。然而在其他情况下，海外属地政府因此失去了对"他们的"银行的控制，并可能根据自身权力范围，对银行以重组形式返回施加限制。印度政府以及加拿大和澳大利亚殖民地的立法机构尤为重视保留对本国银行部门的控制权——例如，印度、澳大利亚和中国特许银行在澳大利亚设立分支的尝试就受到了限制。</span></span></span>

These London and X banks were separate institutions. Three factors are relevant:<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN" style="">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">这些伦敦银行和 X 银行是各自独立的机构。相关因素有三点：</span></span></span>

1.  their overseas or special-interest origins<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN" style="">\
    <span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">它们要么来自海外，要么来自特殊利益背景。</span></span></span>
2.  the debatable nature of exchange operations as 'legitimate' banking, and<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN" style="">\
    <span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">关于外汇操作是否算作“合法”银行业务，这一点本身就颇具争议。</span></span></span>
3.  the initial controversy over the soundness of joint stock banking.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN" style="">\
    <span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">而股份制银行的稳健性，从一开始就备受质疑。</span></span></span>

The India-founded Agra and United Services Bank did join with the London-based Masterman's to permit entry to the Gearing House, but there was opposition and the combined bank failed. In continental Europe, exchange banks trading to the East were consortium banks; attempts in the 1870s by the Deutsche Bank and in the 1890s by the Credit Lyonnais were financially unsuccessful and both withdrew. Only in the immediate aftermath of the First World War did the clearing banks venture overseas —but these operations were themselves kept separate, usually in consequence of a takeover.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN" style="">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">源自印度的阿格拉联合服务银行曾与伦敦的马斯曼银行联手，试图进入清算所体系，但遭遇了反对，最终这家合并后的银行以失败告终。在欧洲大陆，从事东方贸易的外汇银行多为联合银行；德意志银行在 19 世纪 70 年代、里昂信贷银行在 19 世纪 90 年代的尝试均以财务失败告终，二者最终都选择了退出。直到第一次世界大战结束后不久，清算银行才开始涉足海外业务——但这些业务本身往往通过收购方式保持独立运营。</span></span></span>

But were the London and X banks 'multinationals'? If a multinational is defined as a bank operating in two or more jurisdictions, <span class="italic">de facto</span> sovereign in the relevant topics, then it would appear that such banks were multinational. But would this apply if 'X' were a colony still subject to effective Treasury control? Would it apply, in the case of Hong Kong, to the Treaty Ports of China where extrality existed? Probably 'yes' in both cases, since in the latter the consular courts took local practice into consideration and in the former Treasury control was often delayed and reactive.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">但伦敦和 X 银行算得上是“跨国公司”吗？如果跨国公司的定义是在两个或更多司法管辖区内运营、在相关议题上拥有事实主权地位的银行，那么这些银行似乎确实可被称为跨国公司。然而，若“X”是一个仍受英国财政部有效控制的殖民地，这个定义还适用吗？至于香港，若涉及中国境内享有治外法权的通商口岸，这个定义是否依然成立？答案是：在这两种情况下，很可能“是”。因为在后一种情况中，领事法庭会考虑当地惯例；而在前一种情况中，英国财政部的控制往往滞后且被动。</span></span></span>

And yet it seems pretentious to refer to a bank as a multinational merely because it operated between London and X. The Treasury had ruled in 1854:<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">然而，仅仅因为一家银行在伦敦和 X 之间设有业务，就称其为跨国公司，未免有些自命不凡。财政部早在 1854 年就裁定：</span></span></span>

1.  that a bank chartered in India or a colony could not on this basis undertake banking business in the United Kingdom, and<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
    <span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">一家在印度或殖民地注册的银行不能在此基础上在英国开展银行业务，</span></span></span>
2.  an exchange bank should operate only in a specific region clearly defined.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
    <span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">一家兑换银行应仅在明确界定的特定区域内经营。</span></span></span>

Thus it is preferable to think of these banks as 'regional banks'; nevertheless, for purposes of inter-industry consistency, the general definition of a multinational may be utilized.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN" style="">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">因此，将这些银行视为“区域性银行”更为妥当；不过，为了行业间的一致性，也可采用跨国银行的通用定义。</span></span></span>

The restriction on general banking business in England was one <span id="Ch05.xhtml_page_87"></span>factor which may have encouraged the early exchange banks to reestablish in London, but the prohibition was in any case vaguely defined and apparently of no consequence after the enactment of general banking legislation. Overseas restrictions, as in India, could be overcome by the exchange bank registering under a local companies ordinance.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN" style="">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">英格兰对一般银行业务的限制，可能是促使早期汇兑银行在伦敦重新设立的一个因素，但该禁令本身定义模糊，且在一般银行立法出台后显然已无实际影响。而海外限制（如印度）则可通过汇兑银行依据当地公司条例注册来规避。</span></span></span>

The geographical restriction set the pattern for British overseas banking at least until 1945. Exchange banking involved direct risks for three constituencies:<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN" style="">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">地域限制规定了英国海外银行业的发展模式，至少持续到 1945 年。汇兑银行业务对三类群体构成直接风险：</span></span></span>

1.  the shareholders,<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN" style="">\
    <span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">股东、</span></span></span>
2.  the bank's customers, and<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN" style="">\
    <span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">银行客户、</span></span></span>
3.  the bank's noteholders.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN" style="">\
    <span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">银行票据持有人。</span></span></span>

The last two usually included local governments. The shareholders were protected by some form of limited liability (for example, double liability); the bank's customers, to the extent that they were themselves in business, could assess the risk, provided the bank's operations were constrained within an area familiar to them; the noteholders, however, had become, as note holding increased in popularity, totally impersonal - they had no continuing or professional knowledge of the issuing bank. The note issue and the Treasury's reluctant acceptance that government, not the holder, must in the end bear the cost in case of bank failure were key factors in determining the Treasury's 'regional policy'.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN" style="">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">最后两类通常包括地方政府。股东享有某种形式的有限责任保护（例如双重责任）；银行的客户，若其本身从事商业活动，在银行业务局限于其熟悉范围内时，能够评估风险；然而，随着票据持有日益普及，票据持有人已完全失去人格化特征——他们对发钞银行不再有持续或专业的了解。票据发行以及财政部勉强承认——银行倒闭时最终须由政府而非持有人承担损失——成为决定财政部"区域政策"的关键因素。</span></span></span>

Even within the region of the bank's expertise, management could deal directly only with their own community of 'Europeans'; dealing with the local population required intimate knowledge obtainable not simply by learning the language but by association with a local manager who guaranteed all transactions and all local staff; that is, a <span class="italic">compradore.</span> The general custom of granting officer rank only to 'Europeans' forced a separate status for the <span class="italic">compradore</span> and the creation of a bank within a bank. But here again was a limitation: the bank had made contact with the native market through one man whose contacts were restricted and who might inhibit business with other native communities or with other potential but casual native customers.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN" style="">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">即便在该银行擅长的区域内，管理层也只能直接与自己的“欧洲人”社群打交道；要跟本地人做生意，光靠学会语言远远不够，还得有深入的本土知识——这必须通过与一位本地经理合作来获得，他得为所有交易和当地员工担保，说白了就是一位“买办”。而当时普遍只给“欧洲人”授以职员级别的做法，迫使买办处于一种特殊地位，并在银行内部又形成了一个“行中之行”。但这又带来新的限制：银行通过一个人与本地市场建立了联系，而这个人的社交圈子相当有限，反而可能阻碍银行与其他本地社群或潜在的散客开展业务。</span></span></span>

For the typical London and X bank (where 'X' may be either a single country or town or a region) the attractions of a London base were:<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN" style="">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">对于典型的伦敦与 X 银行（其中“X”可以是单一国家、城镇或地区），将总部设在伦敦的吸引力在于：</span></span></span>

1.  the City was an easier political base from which to obtain a charter,<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN" style="">\
    <span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">金融城作为政治基地更便于获取银行特许状，</span></span></span>
2.  if the shareholders were based in England and if the Board were also located there, it would appear logical to have a head office where operations could be more easily controlled.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN" style="">\
    <span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">如果股东位于英国，董事会也设在那里，那么将总部设在便于管控运营的地方似乎合乎逻辑。</span></span></span>

<span id="Ch05.xhtml_page_88"></span>And yet relations within the region were vital. A London and X bank might serve both the trading needs of its founders and first directors and the perceived banking needs of the overseas community. Indeed, in the case of the Hongkong Bank, these needs - extending as they did beyond the direct interests of the founders - established the bank as a 'local bank' from the first. From this base of operations the bank could expand regionally, rationally, and in clearly defined steps.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN" style="">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">然而，区域内关系至关重要。一家伦敦与 X 银行，既能满足其创始人和首批董事的贸易需求，也能满足海外社区的银行业务需求。事实上，以香港银行（Hongkong Bank）为例，这些需求不仅超出了创始人的直接利益范畴，并且从一开始就使该行成为一家“本土银行”。以此为运营基础，该银行得以在区域内理性扩张，并遵循清晰的步骤逐步推进。</span></span></span>

First the bank could nominate agents, perhaps as reward for initial support, but as business expanded, these agents, often themselves interested merchants, proved unsatisfactory - either the merchant could not handle the growing volume of business or the bank could not safely entrust him with the necessary funds. As merchant competition developed, it was unacceptable for the merchant agent to handle his rivals' banking business. These problems might be temporarily overcome by sending an agent, an employee of the bank, to work in the merchant-agent's office, but the only real solution was for the bank to set up its own office. If the location was outside the head office territory, then the bank had become, by the generally-accepted definition, a multinational.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN" style="">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">起初，银行可以指定代理人，或许是为了奖励最初的支持。但随着业务扩张，这些代理人——往往本身就是有利益关系的商人——逐渐暴露出问题：要么无法处理日益增长的业务量，要么银行无法放心地将所需资金托付给他们。随着商人之间的竞争加剧，让代理商人处理竞争对手的银行业务也变得不可接受。派遣银行雇员到代理商的办公室工作或许能暂时解决这些问题，但唯一的根本办法是银行设立自己的办事处。如果这一地点设在总行所在区域之外，按照普遍接受的定义，该银行便成为了一家跨国银行。</span></span></span>

The new office could be referred to either as a 'branch or as an 'agency'; the distinction, however, was far from clear. The Treasury admitted the confusion and stated only that an agency could not issue banknotes, a decision which was not consistently applied. Because the establishment of branches often called for Treasury approval, exchange banks set up what they called 'agencies' unless they intended a major operation from this new base, or unless the local government took note of the bank's charter and itself required the bank to obtain Treasury sanction for a branch. The branch/agency distinction may be further understood in terms of the bank's internal organization; an agency was subordinate to a branch, its manager or 'agent' reporting to that branch. And finally in some jurisdictions - New York State, for example - not only were foreign banks restricted as to the scope and nature of their operations but the term 'agency' had to be applied to their established office.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN" style="">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">新设的办公机构可称为“分行”或“代理处”，但二者界限远非明晰。财政部承认这一混乱，仅表示代理处不得发行钞票，然而这项规定并未一贯执行。由于设立分行通常需经财政部批准，外汇银行在设立新据点时，若无意大规模运营，或当地政府未关注银行章程并自行要求银行就分行设立获取财政部许可，便会设立所谓的“代理处”。分行与代理处的区别还可从银行内部组织角度进一步理解：代理处隶属于分行，其经理或“代理人”需向该分行汇报。最后，在某些司法管辖区（例如纽约州），不仅外国银行的业务范围和性质受到限制，而且其设立的办公机构必须使用“代理处”这一称谓。</span></span></span>

Furthermore whatever the intentions of the directors, an agency originally established for a limited purpose would, unless legally prevented, tend to develop local business - subject only to the manager's ability to garner funds. Branches, consciously established as such, might be assigned their own capital, but were also expected to obtain local funds and to develop their own business, including the establishment of subordinate agencies.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN" style="">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">此外，无论董事们初衷如何，一个最初仅为有限目的而设立的代理机构，除非受到法律约束，往往会倾向于发展本地业务——这仅取决于经理筹集资金的能力。有意设立的分行可能被分配自有资本，但也需在当地筹集资金并发展自身业务，包括建立下级代理机构。</span></span></span>

The geographical restrictions as to branch establishment do not <span id="Ch05.xhtml_page_89"></span>appear to have been contested by bank directors <span class="italic">per se.</span> Furthermore, exchange banks did develop multi-bases (agencies for exchange, which might nevertheless develop some local business) outside their region; for example, the Hongkong Bank might be described as the London, Lyons, Hamburg, and X Bank on the one side and the San Francisco, New York and X Bank on the other; prohibited would be a London, etc and X + Y bank.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN" style="">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">关于分行设立的地域限制，银行董事本身似乎并未提出异议。此外，外汇银行确实在其所在区域之外发展了多基地（即从事外汇业务但可能同时拓展本地业务的代理机构）；例如，汇丰银行可被描述为一边是伦敦、里昂、汉堡及某银行，另一边是旧金山、纽约及某银行；而被禁止的则是伦敦等地与某银行加另一银行的组合。</span></span></span>

That management should acquiesce to such a limitation reflects the problems of branching itself. Gilbart, who had written authoritatively on joint stock banking, urged close supervision of branches, but the Hongkong Bank, for example, operating over great distances, found this impractical and, although it did eventually establish an 'inspector of branches', its main concern was to appoint capable and trustworthy managers. This is consistent with the Treasury assertion that a regional exchange bank resembled a collection of banks operating under a common capital.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN" style="">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">管理层默许这种限制，恰恰反映了分支机构本身的问题。吉尔巴特（Gilbart）曾就股份制银行撰写过权威著作，主张对分支机构进行严密监督；然而，以汇丰银行（Hongkong Bank）为例，由于运营范围横跨遥远地域，这种监督方式并不可行——尽管该行最终设立了"分行巡视员"一职，但其核心举措仍是选派能力出众且值得信赖的经理人。这与财政部的论断不谋而合：区域性外汇银行本质上如同一批在共同资本下运营的银行集合体。</span></span></span>

Nevertheless, control proved to be a major problem. Eastern exchange banks more often failed not because of dishonest managers but because of the managers' failure to undertake sound banking and to resist speculative activities in new surroundings. The Hongkong Bank's crisis in the mid-1870s came from dull (but certainly not dishonest) management; it was saved by brilliant (and lucky) management. This was hardly a system.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN" style="">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">然而，控制却成了一个主要问题。东方汇兑银行的失败更多并非源于管理者的不诚实，而是因为他们在新环境中未能开展稳健的银行业务，也无法抵制投机活动。香港银行在 19 世纪 70 年代中期遭遇的危机源于平庸（但绝非不诚实）的管理；它后来因出色（且幸运）的管理而得以幸存。这几乎称不上是一个体系。</span></span></span>

The solution was the creation of a 'service'. Young men with minimal domestic banking experience were taken into London office, trained in routine banking on-the-job, and sent overseas. It was expected that they would stay with the same bank throughout their careers; loyalty, not auditing and inspection, were to be the keys to integrity; knowledge of an officer's capabilities after years in service under observation would be the basis of selection for advancement. But this system had its limitations. Banking knowledge tended to be restricted both to the organization and to the region - the skills were not easily transferable. Although there were instances of a Hongkong banker resigning, at times with the good wishes of management, to take a senior position in, for example, the Bank of Egypt, these were exceptional. Certainly a chief executive would not welcome the opening of a new region through the transfer of present staff, nor had he the expertise to supervise such diversity; he too was the product of on-the-job training.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN" style="">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">解决方案是建立一种“服务机制”。将一些几乎没有国内银行业务经验的年轻人带入伦敦办事处，让他们在工作中接受常规银行业务的培训，然后派往海外。这些人往往会在同一家银行度过整个职业生涯；诚信的关键在于忠诚，而非审计与监督；经过多年在岗位上的观察，对一名官员能力的了解将成为晋升选拔的基础。但这个制度也有其局限性。银行业务知识往往局限于特定组织与地区——这些技能不易转移。尽管也曾有香港银行家辞职，有时还带着管理层的祝福，去担任例如埃及银行等机构的高级职位，但这些都是例外情况。 当然，首席执行官不会乐于通过调动现有员工来开拓新区域，他也不具备监管如此多样化业务的专长；他也是在职培训的产物。</span></span></span>

Scottish banking has been noted for:<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN" style="">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">苏格兰银行制度以其以下特点著称：</span></span></span>

1.  its branch system,<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN" style="">\
    <span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">其分行系统、</span></span></span>
2.  cash credits, and<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN" style="">\
    <span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">现金信贷、</span></span></span>
3.  its banknotes.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN" style="">\
    <span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">以及其纸币发行。</span></span></span>

<span id="Ch05.xhtml_page_90"></span>The Hongkong Bank professed, on its founding, to be designed on the Scottish system and certainly the right to issue banknotes, the 'profits of issue' as the Treasury put it, appeared a vital privilege to the founders. By the late 1890s, however, banknotes had become in such general use that the demand exceeded the Bank's authorized limits; a further issue was permitted only against 100 per cent specie reserves. The Treasury, which had long renounced any desire to supervise banking institutions but which had felt responsibility so long as a bank's fiduciary issue was significant, could now virtually abdicate its regulatory authority. The exchange banks were, however, not completely 'on their own'; they had local regulations (if any) to contend with.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN" style="">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">汇丰银行在成立时宣称其设计效仿苏格兰体系，而发行纸币的权利——用财政部的话说就是“发行利润”——对创始人而言似乎是一项至关重要的特权。然而，到了 19 世纪 90 年代末，纸币已变得如此普及，以至于需求超过了银行的授权限额；只有在 100%铸币储备的情况下才允许进一步发行。财政部长期放弃监管银行机构的意愿，但只要银行的信用发行规模可观，它就一直感到负有责任，如今它几乎可以放弃其监管权力了。但汇兑银行并非完全“靠自己”——它们还得应对当地法规（如果有的话）。</span></span></span>

Here, moreover, was another argument for regionalism. While the East was on silver, there was a unity on monetary affairs which minimized both cost and risk. If the legendary example of the Scottish manager setting off from his head office with a sack of notes to open a new branch is somewhat exaggerated, there are close examples in the expansion of exchange banks into regional multinationals, following their customers, following trade paths, all ultimately designed to provide the inter-continental links to Europe and to the United States.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN" style="">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">这里还有一个支持地域主义的论据。当东方国家采用银本位制时，货币事务上的统一性将成本和风险降至最低。虽然那个传说中苏格兰经理背着一袋钞票从总部出发去开设新分行的故事可能有些夸张，但汇兑银行在扩张为区域性跨国银行时，确实存在类似的案例——它们追随客户、沿贸易路线拓展，最终目的都是为欧洲和美国提供跨洲际的连通服务。</span></span></span>

The Hongkong Bank, in retaining its head office in the region, was exceptional. The multinational banking system established through the chartered banking system and Treasury policy - the London and X banks - prevailed in principle until the 1950s. Exchange banks had become multinational because:<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN" style="">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">汇丰银行将总部保留在亚洲区域，这实属特例。通过特许银行体制和财政部政策建立的跨国银行体系——即伦敦银行与 X 银行的模式——在原则上一直主导到 20 世纪 50 年代。汇兑银行之所以成为跨国银行，是因为：</span></span></span>

1.  the facilities they needed at the London end were greater than joint stock banks in London were willing to provide unless the exchange banks established an office (usually a head office) in the City - and once this were done, and once joint stock banking became less regulated, the move to full-scale banking for a limited clientele was logical;<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN" style="">\
    <span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">他们在伦敦所需的设施，超出了伦敦本地合股银行愿意提供的范围，除非这些外汇银行在伦敦金融城设立办事处（通常是总部）——而一旦这样做了，并且合股银行业务的监管变得不那么严格，那么向有限客户群提供全面银行业务的转型就顺理成章了；</span></span></span>
2.  the original merchant agents in the region were similarly unable or unwilling to handle the flow of business and an agency became necessary;<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN" style="">\
    <span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">该地区原有的商人代理同样无力或不愿处理日益增长的业务流，因此设立代理机构变得必要；</span></span></span>
3.  control could be maintained internationally by a disciplined 'service';<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN" style="">\
    <span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">通过严格规范的"服务"体系，可以在国际范围内维持控制；</span></span></span>
4.  local customers could nevertheless be accommodated through the <span class="italic">compradore</span> system or its equivalent; and<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN" style="">\
    <span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">本地客户仍可通过买办制度或类似安排得到妥善服务；以及</span></span></span>
5.  the risk of branching across colonial and/or international boundaries was minimized by a common metallic currency, by the existence of extrality, and to a lesser extent by the ability to issue banknotes.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN" style="">\
    <span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">凭借通用金属货币、治外法权的存在，以及在较小程度上依靠发行银行券的能力，跨越殖民地和/或国际边界设立分支的风险得以降到最低。</span></span></span>

<span id="Ch05.xhtml_page_91"></span>Two changes did take place after 1895, both relevant to the last two points. First was the move away from silver in Japan, Southeast Asia, and subsequently in China and Hong Kong itself, and second came the consequent separateness of 'other' colonial banking and monetary systems. The risks had become greater but the expertise had been acquired, and the consequent monetary link to Europe through colonial currency board systems minimized the costs of multinational banking from London —indeed, after 1936 the Hongkong Bank was able to hold its reserves in London rather than dividing them between gold and silver areas.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN" style="">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">1895 年后确实发生了两个变化，两者都与最后两点息息相关。首先是日本、东南亚以及随后中国和香港地区纷纷脱离银本位，其次是随之而来的“其他”殖民地银行和货币体系各自独立。风险与日俱增，但相关专业知识已然积累，于是通过殖民货币局制度与欧洲建立的货币联系，将伦敦跨国银行的成本压至最低——事实上，1936 年后，Hongkong Bank 得以将储备金全部存放于伦敦，而非分散在黄金与白银区域。</span></span></span>

Banking is and has been a highly regulated industry. The overseas structural choices of multinational banks depend on what is permitted by the host territory. The ability of exchange banks to dominate the foreign exchange business and international trade finance in a less developed territory depended on the nature of the local competition and the extent to which the foreign bank was permitted to undertake general banking, including the acceptance of deposits. The first colonial-sponsored local banks did not undertake exchange but did obtain cheap money in the form of government deposits. As these institutions became central banks or, more commonly, as central banks were established, all banks were seen as responsible for acting in conformity with the nation's monetary policy and/or co-operating in the general economic and social policies of the moment.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN" style="">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">银行业始终并持续受到高度监管。跨国银行在海外选择何种组织结构，取决于东道国的政策许可。外汇银行能在欠发达地区主导外汇业务和国际贸易融资，其能力大小既取决于当地竞争格局，也取决于外资银行被允许开展多大范围的普通银行业务（包括吸收存款）。首批由殖民地扶持设立的本地银行虽不经营外汇业务，却通过政府存款获得了廉价资金。随着这些机构演变为中央银行，或更常见的是随着央行体系的确立，所有银行都被要求必须配合国家货币政策，并/或在当前总体经济与社会政策框架下协同运作。</span></span></span>

Nationalists might suspect that foreign banks could not be controlled or that their interests were in conflict with national policy; thus multinationals might find themselves restricted in the light of politically-concerned banking policy or subject to costly activities in the context of, for example, required loans to some particular sector.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">民族主义者可能会怀疑外国银行难以控制，或其利益与国家政策相冲突；因此，跨国公司可能会因受到政治因素影响的银行政策而受到限制，或被迫承担高昂成本，例如被要求向某些特定行业提供贷款。</span></span></span>

The 1950s were, in anticipation of new nations sensitive to overseas control, characterized by the need to rationalize British (and other) overseas banking, by the reopening of the question of geographical spread, and by the parallel geographical diversification of the bank's customers. The question of survival depended in part on the banking policy and economic success of the major host territory (the 'X' of the London and X banks) or of the ability to transfer activities - as the British Bank of the Middle East transferred from Iran to the Gulf. Success also depended on size, rate of growth, access to necessary capital, and flexibility of manage<span id="Ch05.xhtml_page_92"></span>ment. Obviously, in the course of adjustment, some London and X banks would merge or be taken over; each case would depend on the mix of the factors listed.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">20 世纪 50 年代，由于新兴国家对海外控制十分敏感，这一时期的特征表现为：需要对英国（及其他国家）的海外银行业务进行合理化调整、重新探讨地理扩张问题，以及银行客户群体在地理上的多元化并行发展。银行的生存问题在一定程度上取决于主要东道主地区的银行政策与经济成效（即伦敦与 X 银行的“X”），或者取决于能否像英国中东银行从伊朗转移至海湾地区那样，实现业务迁移。此外，规模、增长速度、获取必要资本的渠道以及管理灵活性，同样关乎生存。显然，在调整过程中，部分伦敦与 X 银行可能会合并或被收购；具体情况则取决于上述各因素的组合情况。</span></span></span>

The infinite potential variety of post-1945 multinational banking here suggests using the history of the Hongkong Bank as the 'thread' for the narrative.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">1945 年后跨国银行业务的无限潜在多样性，提示我们可以将汇丰银行的历史作为叙事的“主线”。</span></span></span>

The Hongkong Bank had long operated an agency in San Francisco. To meet the requirements of its Asia-based constituents, the Bank decided to establish a wholly-owned subsidiary incorporated in California to supplement the services it could provide. At the same time the Bank's chief executive instructed the new Hongkong Bank of California to undertake local banking in its own right. The transplanted China-coast bankers proved unsuccessful and Hong Kong head office was too thinly staffed to offer management supervision.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">香港银行（Hongkong Bank）在旧金山经营一家代理处已久。为满足其亚洲客户的需求，该银行决定在加利福尼亚州注册成立一家全资子公司，以补充其所能提供的服务。同时，银行首席执行官指示新成立的加利福尼亚香港银行（Hongkong Bank of California）自行开展本地银行业务。然而，这些来自中国沿海的银行家们水土不服，未能成功，而香港总行人员配备不足，无法提供管理监督。</span></span></span>

There were several underlying problems. Head office had barely been separated from Hong Kong operations, but the San Francisco experience indicated the need for responsible supervision - the tradition of an independent manager sent out to manage a branch 'on his own', subject only to the most general instructions and to occasional inspections, had to be thoroughly reconsidered. Structural change to provide the necessary support and control was essential for true multinational development. The bank reacted and by the late 1970s the essentials - a full inspectorate, technical and specialized services, general managers controlling the flow of information to the chief executive, and a revised constitution for a new executive board of directors - had been completed.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">存在几个根本性问题。总行与香港业务几乎未作区分，但旧金山的经验表明，必须建立负责任的监管机制——那种派遣独立经理人"独自"管理分行、仅接受最笼统指令和偶尔巡查的传统模式，亟需彻底反思。为实现真正的跨国发展，从组织结构上提供必要的支持与管控势在必行。银行随即采取行动，至 1970 年代末，关键要素已悉数到位：完整的监察体系、专业化的技术服务、由总经理把控向首席执行官汇报的信息流，以及为新的执行董事会修订的章程。</span></span></span>

Second, there had to be a major revision of staff policy. Local staff were employed directly by the bank (not through a <span class="italic">compradore), </span> they became eligible for officer status, and contact with the local market was achieved directly - both by the foreign staff and through a 'Chinese manager' who was a normal, senior bank officer. This change had been accomplished in stages by the mid-1960s. Equally importantly, the foreign staff had to be 'upgraded', specialists became essential, and staffs of exchange banks taken over by the Hongkong Bank had to be integrated. (This last proved most difficult; the task may be compared with that of integrating two long-time rival sports teams.) In the process, the full reliance on tradition and team spirit had to be revised to permit general and impersonal control and audit procedures.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">其次，员工政策必须进行重大修订。本地员工由银行直接雇佣（不再通过买办），他们获得了高级职员资格，并通过外籍员工和作为银行高级职员的"本地经理"直接与本地市场建立联系。这一变革在 20 世纪 60 年代中期分阶段完成。同样重要的是，外籍员工必须"升级"，专业人员变得不可或缺，被汇丰银行兼并的外汇银行员工也必须整合（最后一项最为棘手，其难度堪比整合两支长期对立的运动队）。在此过程中，原先完全依赖传统和团队精神的模式必须改革，转而采用通用的非人格化控制和审计流程。</span></span></span>

These developments were mutually reinforcing. The acceptance of local officers and the depersonalization of control permitted the development of a supervisory, policy-making head office, assisted <span id="Ch05.xhtml_page_93"></span>by specialists. The new role of head office In turn permitted the effective management of a structurally more complex multinational operation.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">这些发展相互促进。本地官员的接纳以及控制权的非人格化，使得一个由专家辅助的、负责监督与决策的总部得以形成。而总部的新角色，反过来又推动了对结构更为复杂的跨国业务的有效管理。</span></span></span>

In the process, however, the role of the 'expatriate' officer - and perhaps the image of the bank itself - was changed. Even if the bank had been reluctant to appoint local officers, immigration and work-permit policies would have forced the change, but young expatriates then found it difficult to obtain across-the-counter customer experience at the 'grass-roots' level; in any case they were now more expensive and had other roles to play. The prewar London and X bank, which prided itself on being, say, British, now might have a Chinese or Malay branch manager. Yet these changes were essential if the multinational were to have more than a single-office, trade finance presence in the territory.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">然而，在这一过程中，“外派”人员所扮演的角色——或许还有银行自身的形象——都发生了变化。即便银行此前不愿任命本土员工，移民与工作许可政策也迫使其做出改变；而年轻的外派人员随后发现，他们难以在“基层”获得与客户直接打交道的实战经验；何况，如今他们的成本更高，且需承担其他职责。战前以英资自诩的 London and X 银行，现在可能由华裔或马来裔的分行经理来掌管。然而，若这家跨国银行希望在该地区拥有不止一间办事处或仅从事贸易融资业务，这些变革实属必要之举。</span></span></span>

By 1960 the Hongkong Bank had acquired the Mercantile Bank (of India) and the British Bank of the Middle East. This was part of the rationalization process; India at that time was neither a profitable nor a particularly hospitable base for an exchange bank in the post-Raj era; the Gulf was a growth area but capital support was required. Now the Hongkong Bank was both an operating bank and a bank holding company - the 'Hongkong Bank Group'. Full rationalization of staffs and head offices in Hong Kong took twenty years as traditions - of staffs, procedures, directors and customers - took time to integrate (and, indeed, as the Hongkong Bank itself developed supervisory capabilities).<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">到 1960 年，汇丰银行已经收购了（印度）有利银行和英国中东银行。这是合理化进程的一部分；在后殖民时代，印度对汇兑银行而言既非盈利之地，也非特别宜人的基地；海湾地区虽属增长区域，但需要资本支持。此时的汇丰银行既是一家运营银行，也是一家银行控股公司——即“汇丰银行集团”。人员在完全合理化以及香港总部的整合花了二十年时间——因为员工、流程、董事和客户的传统都需要时间来融合（实际上，汇丰银行自身也在发展监管能力）。</span></span></span>

Similarly the Chartered Bank (of India, Australia and China) merged with the Standard of South Africa to form Standard Chartered Bank.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">同样，（印度、澳大利亚和中国）渣打银行与南非标准银行合并，成为标准渣打银行。</span></span></span>

These developments also reflected the Hongkong Bank's need for diversification geographically; Hong Kong had become too small, the political situation in China prevented growth in the Bank's historical area; India was a high tax country; there were restrictions in the region relative to establishing new branches and, in Sri Lanka, even pressures to divest established 'agencies'; British Borneo could be developed, but it was a lesser market; Japan in the 1950s progressively dispensed with the services of foreign banks as its own banks took on a multinational role; the American operation was in difficulties; other British Commonwealth nations, especially Canada and Australia, were at the time closed to foreign banking. As noted the solution was found in the acquisition of two former London and X banks, one of which was a competitor but with a supplementary branch range especially in India and Malaya, the other was in a new region. The first tests for survival and growth had been passed.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">这些发展也反映出汇丰银行在地域上需要多元化：香港市场已过于狭小，中国政局不稳又限制了银行在其传统区域的增长；印度是个高税率国家；区域内开设新分行存在诸多限制，在斯里兰卡甚至面临剥离已设立“代理机构”的压力；英属婆罗洲虽有发展空间，但市场规模较小；20 世纪 50 年代，随着日本本土银行走向跨国化，日本逐步削减了对外资银行的需求；美国业务陷入困境；其他英联邦国家，尤其是加拿大和澳大利亚，当时对外资银行关闭大门。如前所述，解决方案是通过收购两家前伦敦和 X 银行来实现——其中一家原是竞争对手，但其分行网络尤其在印度和马来亚形成互补；另一家则开拓了新区域。至此，汇丰银行通过了生存与发展的初步考验。</span></span></span>

<span id="Ch05.xhtml_page_94"></span>Postwar survival required growth, and growth required both geographical and product diversification - the latter a continuing challenge as the banking industry was subject to revolutionary technical and conceptual changes especially from the mid-1960s. In 1898 the Hongkong Bank had established the British and Chinese Corporation with Jardine Matheson, but it was now dormant. The Hongkong Bank also had nominee and trustee companies, but its first specialized postwar subsidiary, a finance company, came in the early 1960s. Product diversification took the structural form of subsidiary companies; the technological developments might also require a subsidiary or associate, especially if the facilities were to be shared. Some multinational banks, the Hongkong Bank included, diversified out of finance into communications and transportation, especially shipping. These new departures (new for banks in the British tradition) encouraged internal reorganization, which in turn permitted formal planning and the development of growth strategies.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">战后生存需要增长，而增长则要求在地域和产品两方面实现多元化——后者尤为艰巨，因为自 20 世纪 60 年代中期起，银行业经历了技术及理念上的革命性变革。1898 年，汇丰银行曾与怡和洋行共同组建英中公司，但彼时该公司已处于休眠状态。汇丰银行旗下虽设有代名人及信托公司，但其战后首个专业子公司——一家财务公司——却成立于 60 年代初。产品多元化以子公司的结构形式呈现；技术发展亦可能催生子公司或联营公司，尤其是在设施需共享的情况下。部分跨国银行（包括汇丰银行在内）还从金融领域拓展至通信与运输业，尤以航运为甚。 这些新举措（对英国传统银行而言是新的）鼓励了内部重组，进而使得正式规划和增长战略的制定成为可能。</span></span></span>

As the Hongkong Bank's Califoraian experience indicated, there were risks in developing in unfamiliar situations and, in any case, multinationals were already working through a correspondent bank network. Sound policy suggested the establishment of representative offices; a physical banking presence was needed either when the expected gains offset the loss of correspondent business or when future prospects indicated a policy of 'positioning', that is, being prepared for<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">正如汇丰银行在加州的经历所示，在陌生环境中拓展业务存在风险，且无论如何，跨国公司早已通过代理行网络开展业务。审慎的策略建议先设立代表处；只有当预期收益能够抵消代理行业务损失，或未来发展前景表明需要采取"战略布局"（即提前做好准备）时，才需要设立实体银行机构。</span></span></span>

1.  when and if banking prohibitions should be relaxed, and<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
    <span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">何时以及是否应该放松银行禁令，以及</span></span></span>
2.  the possibility of interstate banking in the United States.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\

Also obvious to both the Hongkong and the Standard Chartered banks was that multinational status required a real banking presence in both the United States and Europe. The latter was particularly important for 'British' or 'British overseas' banks since they were competing with British clearing banks which held overseas subsidiaries. Nevertheless both banks opted to give first priority to the acquisition of an American bank, a priority which, for the Hongkong Bank, would eventually require divestment of the Hongkong Bank of California.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">汇丰银行和渣打银行同样清楚的是，要在跨国地位上名符其实，就必须在欧美两地拥有实质银行业务。欧洲对“英资”或“英属海外”银行尤显关键，因为它们正与持有海外子公司的英国清算银行展开竞争。即便如此，两家银行仍将收购一家美国银行作为首要任务——对汇丰银行而言，这一优先考量最终将迫使其出售加州汇丰银行。</span></span></span>

By the late 1970s the Hongkong Bank's internal reorganization had reached a point where Federal regulators considered the bank able to take responsibility for a major US acquisition. The former on-line operation had developed the complexities of a modern corporate organization. But perhaps the most significant break from tradition, as opposed to innovation, was the new career pattern for successful executives. Formerly management of an <span id="Ch05.xhtml_page_95"></span>overseas branch came as the final assignment before retirement and the manager expected to be left alone to run his domain. In consequence, mid-career executives had been assigned to managerial positions and then brought back to head office, often, but not necessarily, for duty on the country 'desk'. Thus head office is made the focus of the expatriate career, but now it is a head office with more to offer.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">到 20 世纪 70 年代末，汇丰银行的内部重组已达到让美国联邦监管机构认为该行有能力承担一项重大美国收购项目的程度。原有的联机运营体系已发展为具有现代企业组织般的复杂结构。但或许与传统最决裂（而非创新）的举措，在于为成功高管打造的全新职业路径。过去，海外分行管理职位往往是退休前的最后任命，行长们期望能独掌一方天地。因此，处于职业生涯中期的管理人员会被派往管理岗位，随后再调回总部——通常（但不一定）负责相应国家的业务。这样一来，总部便成为外派人员职业生涯的核心，但如今的总部能提供的机遇已远非昔日可比。</span></span></span>

While the above changes were structurally significant in the Bank's traditional areas of operations, including the Gulf, the Californian experience taught the Bank that traditionally-trained bankers found it difficult to transfer their regionally-relevant skills. This suggested that banks acquired in the process of growth required two extreme opposite methods of handling. Those in the tradition of British overseas exchange banks had to be integrated; those elsewhere had to be brought into 'partnership' whether 51 per cent or 100 per cent owned. With the approach to the Marine Midland Bank of New York the stress was on partnership. But the practicality of this policy depends on the to-be-acquired bank having and retaining a sound management - in which case, the bank might be too expensive or unobtainable. The way out of this apparent impasse is found partly in the fact that perception is not everywhere the same, and certainly analysts of Marine Midland underestimated the ability of its management, and partly in the need to offer the acquired bank something it cannot obtain on its own - in this case capital injection to permit the kind of growth which both management and the economy desired.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">上述变化在该行传统业务领域（包括海湾地区）具有结构性意义，但加州的经历让该行认识到，传统培训出身的银行家很难将其区域性经验迁移应用。这表明，在扩张过程中收购的银行需要两种截然不同的处理方式。那些继承英国海外汇兑银行传统的，必须予以整合；而那些其他类型的银行，无论持股 51%还是 100%，都需要建立"合作伙伴"关系。在收购纽约海事米兰银行时，该行特别强调合作伙伴关系。但这一政策的可行性取决于目标银行是否拥有并保持稳健的管理层——若如此，该银行可能因要价过高或无法收购而难以得手。 摆脱这一明显僵局的出路，部分在于感知并非一成不变——显然，Marine Midland 的分析师低估了其管理层的应变能力；另一部分则在于，必须向被收购银行提供其自身无法获得的东西——在此案例中，即是注入资本，以实现管理层与经济体所共同期望的增长。</span></span></span>

'Partnership', however, is not a structural solution for all situations. With their American strategies successful, both the Chartered and the Hongkong banks turned to consideration of a European base. Acquisition of a suitable continental bank was impossible; <span class="italic">de novo</span> branch establishment too expensive. Both looked to an acquisition in the United Kingdom and concluded that the Royal Bank of Scotland Group was the only possible 'doable' deal - and the Chartered made the first approach, calling for an integrated operation directed from Scotland. The Hongkong Bank countered by offering 'partnership', with the Royal Bank playing the role of the Hongkong Bank Group's 'flagship' in Europe, a role which the Royal Bank's directors said they did not wish to play. In the end, and on the recommendation of the Monopolies and Mergers Commission report, both bids failed.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">然而，“合伙关系”并非适用于所有情况的根本解决方案。在美国战略取得成功后，渣打银行和汇丰银行都转而考虑在欧洲设立基地。收购一家合适的欧洲大陆银行是不可能的事；从头设立分支机构又过于昂贵。两家银行都将目光投向英国，认为收购苏格兰皇家银行集团是唯一可行的交易——渣打率先行动，提出以苏格兰为总部的整合运营方案。汇丰银行则提出“合伙关系”作为回应，让苏格兰皇家银行扮演汇丰银行集团在欧洲的“旗舰”角色，但苏格兰皇家银行的董事们表示他们并不想扮演这一角色。最终，根据垄断与兼并委员会的建议，这两项收购要约均以失败告终。</span></span></span>

There followed a lapse while the Hongkong Bank dealt with the situation in Hong Kong. Then, in the pursuit of a British base, the bank developed a new structural approach - its worldwide operations would be rationalized in co-operation with a British <span id="Ch05.xhtml_page_96"></span>clearing bank, the Midland. Seen as a prelude to closer integration, the 14.9 per cent investment through a new capital injection by the Hongkong Bank was a limited decision forced by Bank of England regulations; this was followed by an agreement with Wells Fargo in California. Meanwhile, with the relaxation of restrictive legislation in Canada, the Bank established the Hongkong Bank of Canada as an evolution of finance companies previously positioned. Nevertheless the operation would have had all the makings of an expensive <span class="italic">de novo</span> establishment but for two chance developments:<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">随后，汇丰银行在应对香港局势时出现了一段间歇。接着，为了寻求一个英国基地，该行开发了一种新的组织结构——它将与英国结算银行米特兰银行合作，对其全球业务进行合理化调整。汇丰通过注资获得 14.9%的股权，被视为迈向更紧密整合的前奏，但这实则是在英格兰银行规定下被迫做出的有限决策；随后，该行又与加州富国银行达成了协议。与此同时，随着加拿大放宽限制性立法，汇丰将之前布局的金融公司转型为加拿大汇丰银行。不过，若非两个偶然事件，这一运作本来完全可能沦为代价高昂的新设机构：</span></span></span>

1.  the HBC took over the business of the problem Bank of British Columbia, and<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
    <span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">汇丰银行接手了问题缠身的不列颠哥伦比亚银行业务，</span></span></span>
2.  there was the takeover by the agreement noted above of the Midland Bank's Canadian operations.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\

The structure of multinationals is not only a consequence of corporate planning - it has a political and regulatory component. On occasion, as in Saudi Arabia, the price of continued full banking operations may be the establishment of a joint venture operation with a management licence; it may require, as had been planned in Malaysia, that a separate company be established with a majority local shareholding; to achieve the benefits of limited interstate banking, Edge Act corporations were established; to provide for a sounder, modern handling of the bank's properties, holding companies would be established in the Bahamas and elsewhere.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">跨国公司的结构不仅仅是企业规划的结果——它还包含着政治和监管因素。有时，如在沙特阿拉伯，要继续全面开展银行业务，代价可能是成立一家附带管理许可的合资企业；在马来西亚，则可能要求（正如之前计划的那样）成立一家本地股东占多数的独立公司；为了享受有限跨州银行业务的好处，成立了 Edge Act 公司；为了更稳妥、更现代地管理银行资产，则在巴哈马及其他地方设立了控股公司。</span></span></span>

The complexity of a multinational bank's organization chart with, in the case of the Hongkong Bank, some 400 subsidiary and other companies, is at least in part the consequence of regulatory requirements and/or tax provisions - on this last point, a series of holding companies may permit 'down-streaming' of dividends, being able to hold them, with beneficial consequences, in a low tax area. Management, however, can decide which of the technical and financial operations should be separately incorporated, each decision having some element of concern relative to the degree of integration necessary for efficient operations and the likelihood of the particular service being shared (as noted before) with non-Group customers. Nominee and trustee companies must be separately established and incorporated in each country, for example.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">跨国银行组织架构的复杂性，以汇丰银行为例，其拥有约 400 家子公司及其他附属公司，至少部分源于监管要求和/或税收规定——就后者而言，通过一系列控股公司或许能够实现股息“下流”（down-streaming），从而在低税率地区持有这些股息，并带来有利的税务效果。然而，管理层可以决定哪些技术及财务业务应单独成立公司，每项决策都需兼顾高效运作所需的整合程度，以及特定服务（如前所述）与非集团客户共享的可能性。例如，代名人公司及受托人公司必须在每个国家单独设立并注册成立。</span></span></span>

Another approach is to note the existence of the international staff, the core of key executives eligible for appointment to the highest positions. Operations which do not require their involvement may be separately incorporated, especially if they have their own particular professional standards. Some banking activities previously excluded by regulations can now be handled by the <span id="Ch05.xhtml_page_97"></span>parent corporation while, at the other extreme, services commonly considered as part of a bank's services can be detached if a payment is newly required for such services - especially if they are likely to be used by persons not otherwise dealing with the bank. Control is maintained at the minimum by composition of the subsidiary's board of directors.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">另一种方法是注意到国际员工的存在，他们是可被任命到最高职位的关键高管核心。不需要他们参与的业务可以单独组建公司，尤其是当这些业务有自己特定的专业标准时。一些先前被法规排除的银行业务现在可以由母公司处理；而在另一个极端，通常被视为银行服务一部分的服务，如果对这些服务新增加收费，则可以剥离——尤其是当这些服务可能被其他不与银行交易的人使用时。通过子公司的董事会构成，可以将控制维持在最低限度。</span></span></span>

III<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">三</span></span></span>

The structural options are multiple, and the ultimate decisions and thus the corporate chart at any given time are a consequence of history. The old retiree after a tour of the wonders of head office turned to the Chairman's messenger, himself old in the service, and said, 'Tom, when you and I were young, just the two of us did all this together!'<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">结构性选择众多，最终的决策——以及任何时刻的公司架构——都是历史的产物。那位退休老人在参观完总部的种种奇观后，转向主席的助理——他自己也已服务多年——说道：“汤姆，当年你我还年轻时，就咱们两个人，一起干成了这所有的一切！”</span></span></span>

What indeed is left of the London and X bank, with its juniors eager for the playing fields, its seniors steeped in the traditions of European life on the China coast, of the <span class="italic">compradores</span> keeping the doors open to the merchants of China, to those very people few exchange bankers would ever know? What, in today's multinational banking institution, is left of its history? Does it have anything to offer other than claims to greater efficiency or service —or being ahead of its rivals with this or that gadget, this or that new service? The young Chinese Peking manager of the Hongkong Bank had never heard of E. Guy Hillier, the long-time manager (1885-1924) who negotiated the great China loans; a local junior was surprised to know that Sir Thomas Jackson, despite his prominence as a statue in the city's central square, worked for 'us'. On the other hand the Cameron Highlands branch is no longer designed to provide a rest cure for an expatriate manager in poor health; it is run by a Malaysian officer determined to finance the ever-growing temperate-climate vegetable crops and the tea plantations, opening new markets as far away as Tokyo.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">伦敦 X 银行如今还剩下些什么？它的年轻职员们一心想着玩乐，资深员工沉浸在中国沿海的欧洲生活传统里，买办们则为中国的商人敞开大门——那些连外汇银行家都几乎不认识的人。在今天这家跨国银行机构中，它的历史又还剩下多少？除了宣称自己更高效、服务更周到，或者靠某个小玩意儿、某项新服务领先对手之外，它还能提供什么？香港银行那位年轻的北京经理从未听说过 E. Guy Hillier——这位长期担任经理（1885-1924）的人曾谈判过中国巨额贷款；而当地一名初级职员惊讶地发现，尽管托马斯·杰克逊爵士在城市中心广场立有雕像，但他居然是为“我们”工作的。 另一方面，金马仑高原分行已不再是为身体欠佳的外籍经理提供疗养服务；如今由一位马来西亚官员管理，他决心为日益增长的温带蔬菜作物和茶园提供资金支持，并开拓远至东京的新市场。</span></span></span>

Is it possible that the customers have a better 'feel for the old bank than its modern style employees - both clerks and executives?<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">难道客户们对这家老银行的"感觉"，比那些现代风格的员工——无论普通职员还是高管——还要更准吗？</span></span></span>

The problem is not one of sentiment but of identity. Once the London and X bank has become one of the world's great financial groups, what exactly is it? Certainly its business no longer depends on the signature of a member of the British staff. Nevertheless the Hongkong Bank is a British overseas bank, an Eastern exchange bank; its representative offices in Brazil, its branches in Chile, and its new manifestations in Australia, Canada, and the United States do not make the image meaningless - unlike the Canadian Pacific <span id="Ch05.xhtml_page_98"></span>which so outgrew its image that it feared its own full name.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">问题不在于情感，而在于身份认同。一旦伦敦与 X 银行成为世界级金融集团之一，它究竟算什么？当然，它的业务不再依赖于英国职员的签名。尽管如此，Hongkong Bank 仍是一家英国海外银行、一家东方汇兑银行；其在巴西的代表处、在智利的分行，以及在澳大利亚、加拿大和美国的新面貌，并未使这一形象失去意义——不像 Canadian Pacific 那样，因为发展得过于庞大，以至于对自己的全名都感到恐惧。</span></span></span>

A more reasoned approach would be to say that since the early 1960s Hong Kong business has expanded to these same diverse areas, and as Hong Kong was once a hub for trade with Saigon, Manila, the Straits, China and Japan, so now its bank services the Pacific Rim without necessary loss of identity. For every objection there is a counter. Even in a building designed for the twenty-first century (but on the site of the bank's original office at 1 Queen's Road Central), there is room for the pictures of past chief executives, Some would say there is a corporate identity which, intangibly, shapes or influences the way of doing business, of reacting to events, of handling constituents. Structural change, it must be argued, cannot be made to accommodate historical continuity, but, having said that, the history yet seems to have survived, its presence is still felt. The bankers speak of it, the public affairs department writes of it, and in the archives the story is preserved.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">一种更理性的说法是：自 20 世纪 60 年代初以来，香港商业已扩展到同样多元化的领域；正如香港曾是西贡、马尼拉、海峡殖民地、中国和日本贸易的枢纽，如今其银行业服务环太平洋地区，却并未丧失自身特色。每个反对观点都有反驳之词。即便在一座为 21 世纪设计的建筑中（但选址于原银行总部旧址——皇后大道中 1 号），仍留有空间悬挂历任行政总裁的画像。有人认为，一种无形的企业身份会塑造或影响经营方式、应对事件的方式以及处理客户关系的方式。必须指出，结构性变革无法迁就历史连续性，但即便如此，历史似乎依然存续，其存在感仍可感知。银行家们谈论它，公共事务部门书写它，而在档案中，这段故事被完整保存。</span></span></span>

This chapter is based on research into the history of the Hongkong and Shanghai Banking Corporation. The Hongkong Bank provides the basic story and key variations are noted.
