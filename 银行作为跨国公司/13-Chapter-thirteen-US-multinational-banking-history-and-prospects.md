Thomas F. Huertas<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN" style="">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">托马斯·F·韦尔塔斯</span></span></span>

In the history (and future) of banks as multinationals, US multinational banking plays a central role. 'US banks' have been among the most active banks in developing multinational networks, and 'non-US banks' have generally viewed the US as one of the most important targets for their foreign expansion. This chapter traces the history of US multinational banking from 1914.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN" style="">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">在银行作为跨国企业的历史（及未来）中，美国跨国银行扮演着核心角色。"美国银行"一直是发展跨国网络最活跃的银行之一，而"非美国银行"通常将美国视为其海外扩张最重要的目标之一。本章追溯了自 1914 年以来美国跨国银行的发展历程。</span></span></span>

Banks as multinationals<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN" style="">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">银行作为跨国企业</span></span></span>
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

In general, value-adding activities take place in firms when the benefits of transacting within the firm are greater than the benefits of transacting in the market.<sup>1</sup> As the sources of added value or the costs of transacting change, so will the balance of activity conducted in firms <span class="italic">vis-á-vis</span> activity conducted in the market.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN" style="">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">一般而言，当企业内部的交易收益大于市场交易收益时，增值活动便会在企业内部进行。 <sup>1</sup> 随着增值来源或交易成本的变化，企业内部活动与市场活动之间的平衡也会随之改变。</span></span></span>

Both banks and multinationals are examples of this paradigm at work. Banks provide essentially two types of services: a transactions or administration service and an intermediation service. The administration service encompasses effecting payments, clearing and settling securities trades, securities custody, etc. At one extreme, cash transactions compete with the services offered by banks; at the other, so do computerized business-to-business trade payments. The intermediation service in effect links seekers of capital with suppliers of capital by transforming the characteristics (maturity, size, liquidity, risk) desired by the former into ones acceptable to the latter. Again, a range of market activities, from trade credit to direct placement of securities by issuers with investors, compete with the services supplied by banks.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN" style="">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">银行和跨国公司都是这种范式的运作实例。银行主要提供两类服务：交易/行政服务与中介服务。行政服务涵盖支付执行、证券交易清算结算、证券托管等业务。在服务链的一端，现金交易与银行服务形成竞争；另一端，企业间的电子化贸易支付同样构成挑战。中介服务本质上是通过将资本需求方期望的特征（期限、规模、流动性、风险）转化为资本供应方可接受的形式，实现供需双方的对接。同样，从商业信用到发行人直接向投资者配售证券等一系列市场活动，都在与银行传统服务展开竞争。</span></span></span>

The theory of multinational enterprises is particularly concerned with the 'geopolitical' scope of the firm.<sup>2</sup> When will a firm own or control value-adding activities in two or more countries — i.e. when will a firm become a multinational? What proportion of the firms in an industry will become multinational, and what share of the <span id="Ch013.xhtml_page_249"></span>industry's total output will multinational firms provide? The answers to these questions depend to a large extent on whether it is more efficient for a single firm to control activities in many countries or for several independent firms to trade across borders with each other in the market. Secondarily, given that a firm is a multinational, in which countries does it choose to locate its production, and how does its 'home' country affect its ability to compete?<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN" style="">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">跨国企业理论特别关注企业的“地缘政治”范围。 <sup>2</sup> 企业何时会拥有或控制在两个及以上国家的增值活动——即企业何时会成为跨国公司？一个行业中会有多大比例的企业成为跨国公司，这些跨国企业又会在行业总产出中占据多大份额？这些问题的答案很大程度上取决于：由单一企业控制多国活动，还是由多家独立企业在市场中进行跨境交易，哪一种方式更高效。其次，如果一家企业已是跨国公司，它会选择在哪些国家布局生产，而其“母国”又如何影响其竞争力？</span></span></span>

This chapter attempts to answer these questions for multinational banking organizations operating in the United States. The chapter covers both 'US bank MNEs' and 'non-US' bank MNEs', according to whether the parent company is headquartered inside or outside the United States.<sup>3</sup><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">本章旨在回答在美国运营的跨国银行组织所面临的这些问题。根据母公司总部是否位于美国境内，本章将分别涵盖“美国银行跨国企业”和“非美国银行跨国企业”。 <sup>3</sup> </span></span></span>

The history falls into two periods: a 'local' period from 1914 to 1960 and a 'global' one from 1960 onward. In the first, multinational banking was the exception; in the latter it became the rule, in the sense that practically every major US bank had one or more foreign offices and practically every major foreign bank had operations in the United States. The chapter sketches each of these periods in turn and then examines the causes behind the rise of global banking over the past thirty years.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">这段历史可分为两个时期：1914 年至 1960 年的“本土化”时期，以及 1960 年至今的“全球化”时期。在第一个时期，跨国银行业务尚属例外；而在第二个时期，它已成为常态——几乎每家美国大型银行都设有一家或多家海外分支机构，同时几乎所有大型外国银行都在美国开展业务。本章将依次概述这两个时期，随后探讨过去三十年间全球银行业兴起的背后原因。</span></span></span>

The ‘local’ era: 1914-1960<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">“本土化”时代：1914-1960 年</span></span></span>
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The history of US multinational banking from 1914 to 1960 is essentially the history of the foreign operations of a handful of US banks and of the US operations of a small number of foreign banks. Broadly speaking, to the extent that US banks conducted operations with foreign entities or that foreign banks conducted operations with US entities, they did so by market mechanisms, such as a correspondent bank network, rather than internally through offices of their own firm.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">1914 年至 1960 年美国跨国银行业的历史，本质上就是少数美国银行的海外业务以及少数外国银行在美国业务的历史。总体而言，美国银行与外国实体开展业务，或外国银行与美国实体开展业务，都是通过市场机制（如代理行网络）来实现的，而非通过它们自身设立在外的分支机构。</span></span></span>

### US bank MNEs<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">美国银行跨国企业</span></span></span>

As of year-end 1913 only six US banks had branches abroad, and most banks, including all national banks, had no branches at all. In contrast to the United Kingdom and Canada, where banks were expanding throughout their domestic market via branching and acquisition, US banks were confined to their home state, and most states restricted banks to a single office within that state. By 1960 little had changed. Only eight US banks had foreign branches, and most US banks, including those in New York, still could not branch freely throughout their home state, much less across the <span id="Ch013.xhtml_page_250"></span>country. Hence, US banks were overwhelmingly small institutions that conducted most of their business locally. To the extent that if the typical US bank wished to provide services to a customer in another city, it generally relied upon a network of correspondent banks.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">截至 1913 年底，全美仅有 6 家银行设有海外分行，而大多数银行（包括所有国民银行）根本没有分支机构。与通过开设分行和并购方式在国内市场扩张的英国、加拿大银行不同，美国银行被限制在本州经营，且大多数州规定银行只能在本州内设立单一营业网点。到了 1960 年，情况几乎毫无改观。当时仅 8 家美国银行设有海外分行，包括纽约银行在内的大多数银行，甚至无法在所在州自由开设分行，更遑论跨州经营。因此，美国银行绝大多数是仅在本地开展业务的小型机构。若普通美国银行想为其他城市客户提供服务，通常需依赖代理行网络。</span></span></span>

Of the US banks with foreign branches in 1960, all did a significant US business as well as operating offshore. There had been a brief flurry of offshore consortium banks established in the years 1914 to 1920, but the severe downturn of 1920-21, together with these banks' divided ownership, quickly pushed these offshore banks either into liquidation or into a merger with another bank.<sup>4</sup><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">在 1960 年拥有海外分行的美国银行中，所有银行都在经营海外业务的同时，也在美国本土开展了大量业务。1914 年至 1920 年间曾短暂涌现一批离岸联合银行，但 1920-21 年的严重经济衰退，加上这些银行所有权分散，迅速迫使它们要么清算，要么与其他银行合并。 <sup>4</sup> </span></span></span>

During this period Citibank had by far the largest network, and it was the US bank most committed to international banking. Established in New York in 1812, the bank opened its first branch in Buenos Aires, Argentina, in 1914 and quickly built up a worldwide branch network through acquisition and <span class="italic">de novo</span> expansion. By 1916 it had begun to think of itself as a global bank, and for most, if not all, of this period well over half of all foreign branches of US banks were branches of Citibank.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">在此期间，Citibank 拥有迄今为止最大的网络，并且是最致力于国际业务的美国银行。该行于 1812 年在纽约成立，1914 年在阿根廷布宜诺斯艾利斯开设了第一家分行，并通过收购和新建扩张迅速建立起全球分行网络。到 1916 年，它已开始将自己视为一家全球性银行，而在此阶段的大部分时间（若非全部），美国银行所有海外分行中超过一半都是 Citibank 的分行。</span></span></span>

The passage of the Federal Reserve Act, together with the advent of the First World War, opened the door to foreign expansion by Citibank and other US banks. The Federal Reserve Act permitted nationally chartered banks with over \$1 million in capital to establish foreign branches, and subsequent amendments in 1916 and 1919 permitted US banks to acquire subsidiaries engaged in foreign banking.<sup>5</sup> The First World War reduced the ability of British and German banks to compete overseas — just at the time when non-financial US multinationals were expanding rapidly into Latin America, Russia and Asia.<sup>6</sup><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">美联储法案的通过，加上第一次世界大战的爆发，为花旗银行及其他美国银行打开了海外扩张的大门。该法案允许资本超过 100 万美元的全国性银行设立海外分行，随后 1916 年和 1919 年的修正案进一步允许美国银行收购从事海外业务的子公司。 <sup>5</sup> 第一次世界大战削弱了英国和德国银行的海外竞争能力——恰逢此时，非金融类的美国跨国公司正迅速向拉丁美洲、俄罗斯和亚洲扩张。 <sup>6</sup> </span></span></span>

Customers pushed Citibank to take advantage of this opening. For example, James A. Farrell, President of US Steel, wrote to Citibank in 1914 to ask that it establish a branch in Argentina. In return, US Steel would make available to the bank its credit files on Argentine importers and would make a large deposit in the bank. Du Pont made a similar request in connection with its importation of nitrates from Chile.<sup>7</sup> Frank Vanderlip, President of Citibank, saw that such a foreign service could enable Citibank to differentiate itself from the other large wholesale banks competing for business from the nation's largest corporations. Regarding the opening of the first foreign branches Vanderlip commented:<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">客户纷纷推动花旗银行抓住这一机遇。例如，美国钢铁公司总裁詹姆斯·A·法雷尔于 1914 年致信花旗银行，请求其在阿根廷设立分行。作为回报，美国钢铁公司将向该行提供其关于阿根廷进口商的信用档案，并在该行存入大笔资金。杜邦公司也因从智利进口硝酸盐提出了类似请求。 <sup>7</sup> 花旗银行总裁弗兰克·范德利普意识到，开展此类海外业务能够帮助花旗银行在与争夺国内大型企业客户的其他大型批发银行竞争中脱颖而出。谈及首批海外分行的设立，范德利普评论道：</span></span></span>

<div class="quotes">

I do not expect much profit out of it. Indeed, I shall not be surprised if there is something less than any profit, but I hope to get a very considerable return by offering facilities that other <span id="Ch013.xhtml_page_251"></span>banks cannot offer to exporters, and thus attract their accounts to the City Bank.<sup>8</sup><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">我并不指望从中获得多少利润。事实上，即使略有亏损，我也不会感到意外，但我希望通过提供其他银行无法给予出口商的便利服务，从而吸引他们将账户转到花旗银行，以此获得可观的回报。 <sup>8</sup> </span></span></span>

</div>

In fact, the foreign branches brought Citibank both additional corporate accounts and significant profits in the early years. In 1915 Citibank acquired a controlling interest in the International Banking Corporation, the largest and most successful of the US offshore banks in existence in 1914. By the end of 1917 Citibank had a total of 34 foreign branches, and the foreign branches accounted for approximately 20 per cent of the bank's total assets and total profits. This early success led Vanderlip to change his concept, not only of the foreign branches' role, but also of the bank itself. No longer were the foreign branches to be an add-on service; instead they were the foundation of an 'all world' bank.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">事实上，海外分行在早期为花旗银行带来了大量企业账户和可观利润。1915 年，花旗银行收购了国际银行公司（International Banking Corporation）的控股权——这家公司是 1914 年时美国规模最大、业绩最出色的海外银行。到 1917 年底，花旗银行共拥有 34 家海外分行，这些海外分行贡献了该行总资产和总利润的约 20%。这一早期的成功让范德利普（Vanderlip）重新审视自己对于海外分行乃至整个银行战略的定位。海外分行不再仅仅是附加服务，而是成为打造"全球性银行"的基石。</span></span></span>

The rapid establishment and early success of this global network served to sustain Citibank's commitment to multinational banking in the years after 1917. In 1918 the bank's branches in Russia were nationalized; for a time it appeared that the bank would not only lose the capital that it had invested in the branches, but that it would also be liable to pay ruble deposits in New York at the pre-revolution rate of exchange. In 1921 sugar prices collapsed; the bank's branches in Cuba suffered severe losses, impairing the bank's overall capital. As a result of the first episode, the Bank's president lost his job; as a result of the second, so did his successor.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">这个全球网络的迅速建立和早期成功，使得花旗银行在 1917 年后的多年里，始终坚定地致力于跨国银行业务。1918 年，该行在俄罗斯的分行被国有化；一度看来，花旗不仅会损失在这些分行投入的资本，还可能需要按革命前的汇率，用纽约的美元来兑付卢布存款。1921 年，糖价暴跌；该行在古巴的分行遭受严重损失，拖累了整体资本。经历了第一起事件，花旗银行行长丢了饭碗；而第二起事件，则让他的继任者也黯然下台。</span></span></span>

The branches, however, remained open. Indeed, during the 1920s the bank further expanded the network via <span class="italic">de novo</span> expansion and the acquisition of the assets of the Asia Banking Corporation, another US-headquartered offshore bank. At the end of 1930, Citibank had 97 foreign branches, two-thirds of them in Latin America.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">然而，这些分行依然继续运营。事实上，在 20 世纪 20 年代，花旗银行通过自主扩张，以及收购另一家总部位于美国的离岸银行——亚洲银行公司的资产，进一步扩展了这一网络。到 1930 年底，花旗银行共设有 97 家海外分行，其中三分之二在拉丁美洲。</span></span></span>

More losses followed in the Depression years (including new losses on Cuban sugar loans). In 1933, as in 1921, the Board considered closing the foreign branches, but in each case decided that the network was too valuable to lose.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">大萧条时期又接连遭受了更多的损失（其中包括古巴糖业贷款的新亏损）。1933 年，与 1921 年如出一辙，董事会再次考虑关闭海外分行，但两次都认定这个网络价值连城，难以割舍。</span></span></span>

However, economic pressures and war forced Citibank to halve the actual number of branches and to forgo what would otherwise have been attractive expansion opportunities, such as opening a branch in Switzerland, By 1944 the number of foreign branches had fallen to 45. Following the war, Citibank moved quickly back into China and somewhat more slowly into Europe. Although the Chinese branches were nationalized in 1949, the bank's foreign branches rose steadily in number from 45 in 1944 to 76 in 1959, before sinking to 70 in 1960 following the nationalization of the Cuban branches in that year.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">然而，经济压力与战争迫使花旗银行将实际分行数量削减了一半，并放弃了本可大展拳脚的扩张机遇，比如在瑞士开设分行。到 1944 年，海外分行数量已降至 45 家。战后，花旗银行迅速重返中国，对欧洲的恢复则稍显迟缓。尽管在华分行于 1949 年被国有化，该行的海外分行数量仍从 1944 年的 45 家稳步增长至 1959 年的 76 家，随后在 1960 年因古巴分行被国有化而降至 70 家。</span></span></span>

<span id="Ch013.xhtml_page_252"></span>At other US multinational banks, foreign branches figured less prominently in the bank's strategy and performance than at Citibank. No other US bank could lay claim to having a global network of branches, and no other US bank thought of itself as a global bank. Chase and Bank of America came closest in terms of number of branches, although each viewed themselves as primarily a domestic bank. In 1960 each had a sizeable number of foreign branches spread through many different countries around the world.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">在美国其他跨国银行中，海外分行在其战略和业绩中的重要性远不及花旗银行。没有哪家美国银行敢说自己拥有全球分行网络，也没有哪家银行自视为全球性银行。大通银行和美国银行在分行数量上最为接近，尽管它们各自主要将自己定位为国内银行。1960 年，这两家银行都拥有相当数量的海外分行，遍布全球许多不同国家。</span></span></span>

In contrast, the Bank of Boston and Morgan Guaranty had small, rather specialized foreign branch systems. The former's was largely in Argentina, and was originally prompted by the bank's close relationships with New England textile firms who imported large quantities of wool from Buenos Aires.<sup>9</sup><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">相比之下，波士顿银行和摩根保证信托公司的海外分行系统规模较小且专业性较强。波士顿银行的海外分行主要分布在阿根廷，最初是因其与从布宜诺斯艾利斯大量进口羊毛的新英格兰纺织公司关系密切而设立的。 <sup>9</sup> </span></span></span>

Morgan Guaranty's network was centred on London and Paris, the financial capitals of Europe. The two firms, J.P. Morgan & Co. and Guaranty Trust Company, had merged in 1959. Each had had offices or affiliations in London and Paris for decades. Indeed, the Morgan connection with London dated back to the mid nineteenth century and the J.S. Morgan bank; that in Paris dated from the late nineteenth century and the Morgan, Harjes partnership. At the turn of the century J.P. Morgan was the senior partner in each of the two European as well as in the American house.<sup>10</sup> Guaranty had long ties to London and Paris as well. Its London branch was already in existence in 1913. It acquired its Paris branch in the 1920s from the American Foreign Banking Corporation, another US-based offshore bank, when that bank wound up its operations in the early 1920s.<sup>11</sup><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">摩根担保公司的网络主要集中在欧洲金融中心伦敦和巴黎。这两家公司——J.P.摩根公司（J.P. Morgan & Co.）与担保信托公司（Guaranty Trust Company）——于 1959 年合并。此前数十年间，它们各自在伦敦和巴黎都设有办事处或分支机构。事实上，摩根与伦敦的渊源可追溯至 19 世纪中期及 J.S.摩根银行；其在巴黎的联系则始于 19 世纪末的摩根-哈杰斯合伙公司。世纪之交时，J.P.摩根不仅是美国本土公司的资深合伙人，也是这两家欧洲公司的高级合伙人。 <sup>10</sup> 担保信托公司同样与伦敦和巴黎有着长期联系。它早在 1913 年就设立了伦敦分行，并于 20 世纪 20 年代从美国外国银行公司（另一家总部位于美国的离岸银行）手中收购了其在巴黎的分行——当时这家银行刚刚在 20 年代初结束运营。 <sup>11</sup> </span></span></span>

But the banks mentioned here were the exception rather than the rule. Of the 13,126 US-insured commercial banks in operation at the end of 1960, only eight had foreign branches. Although the banks with foreign branches tended to be the largest US banks, the assets of their foreign branches amounted to little more than 1 per cent of the total assets of all insured commercial banks. Overall, foreign business was distinctly secondary to domestic, even at the large banks with foreign branches.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">但这里提到的银行是例外而非普遍情况。在 1960 年底运营的 13,126 家美国受保商业银行中，只有 8 家设有海外分行。尽管设有海外分行的银行往往是美国最大的银行，但其海外分行的资产总额仅占所有受保商业银行总资产的 1%多一点。总体而言，海外业务明显次于国内业务，即使在那些设有海外分行的大型银行也是如此。</span></span></span>

### Non-US bank MNEs in the US<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">非美国银行跨国公司在美业务</span></span></span>

Just as there were few US bank MNEs before 1960, so too were there few non-US bank MNEs operating in the US. Although there was a long history of non-US banks establishing agencies in the US, and of foreign individuals owning shares and in some cases controlling interests in US banks, practically no non-US bank <span id="Ch013.xhtml_page_253"></span>MNEs operated in the US in 1913. By 1960 little had changed. Few foreign hanks had established branches or subsidiaries in the US, although there was no barrier in federal law against their doing so.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">1960 年之前，美国本土的银行类跨国公司屈指可数，同样，在美国运营的非美国银行跨国公司也寥寥无几。尽管非美国银行在美国设立代理机构的历史悠久，外国个人也持有美国银行的股份，有时甚至掌握控股权，但到 1913 年，实际上几乎没有非美国银行跨国公司在美开展业务。到了 1960 年，情况变化甚微。尽管联邦法律并未禁止外国银行在美国设立分行或子公司，但当时这样做的外国银行凤毛麟角。</span></span></span>

The ‘global’ era: 1960-1989<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">全球时代：1960-1989 年</span></span></span>
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

In the period after 1960 US multinational banking blossomed. The number of US banks with foreign branches increased by leaps and bounds, and the number of non-US bank MNEs entering the US grew just as rapidly. By the end of 1988 the majority of US banking business was conducted by MNEs competing in a global marketplace.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">1960 年之后，美国跨国银行业迎来了蓬勃发展。拥有海外分行的美国银行数量激增，而进入美国市场的非美国银行跨国公司增长同样迅猛。到 1988 年底，美国银行业的大部分业务已由在全球市场竞争的跨国公司主导。</span></span></span>

### US bank MNEs<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">美国银行跨国公司</span></span></span>

Both the number of US banks with foreign branches and the assets of such branches increased rapidly from 1960 to 1983, before declining in real terms during the next four years.<sup>12</sup> At year end 1986, 151 US banks had a total of 899 foreign branches and 860 foreign subsidiaries. Total assets at these branches and subsidiaries amounted to approximately \$420 billion, or about 25 per cent of the total assets of US hanks with foreign offices and about 14 per cent of the assets of all US commercial banks (see [Table 13.1](#Ch013.xhtml_fig0023)).<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">从 1960 年到 1983 年，设有海外分行的美国银行数量及其分行的资产均迅速增长，但随后四年实际规模有所下降。 <sup>12</sup> 截至 1986 年底，151 家美国银行共拥有 899 家海外分行和 860 家海外子公司。这些分行和子公司的总资产约为 4200 亿美元，占设有海外机构的美国银行总资产的约 25%，以及所有美国商业银行总资产的约 14%（见表 13.1）。</span></span></span>

<span id="Ch013.xhtml_page_254"></span>Broadly speaking, US banks had three rationales for MNE formation and expansion: better funding for domestic activities, participation in emerging global capital markets, and participation in local corporate and consumer markets for banking and other financial services.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">总体而言，美国银行形成和扩张跨国公司的理由有三：为国内活动提供更好的资金支持、参与新兴的全球资本市场，以及进入本地企业和消费者银行及其他金融服务市场。</span></span></span>

In explaining the growth of numbers of banks and branches, the first rationale seems to have dominated. Evidence to this effect is the large share of offshore banking centres in the Bahamas and Cayman Islands in the total assets of foreign branches of US banks, and the high share of such foreign branches' assets that are claims on the bank's head office. For many US bank MNEs such an offshore branch is their only office outside the US.<sup>13</sup><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">在解释银行及其分行数量增长的原因时，第一个理由似乎占据了主导地位。佐证这一点的证据是，巴哈马和开曼群岛的离岸银行中心在美国银行国外分行的总资产中占有很大份额，而这些国外分行资产中，又有很大一部分是对其银行总部的债权。对于许多美国银行跨国公司而言，这样的离岸分行是它们在美国以外的唯一办事处。 <sup>13</sup> </span></span></span>

The surge in US bank MNEs coincided with the birth and development of the Eurodollar market. This offered US banks an opportunity to raise dollars overseas free from the constraint of Regulation Q, a limit on the rate of interest that US banks could pay on time deposits in their domestic offices. Since this regulation did not apply overseas, many US banks used the Eurodollar market to raise funds that could be re-lent to the domestic branches to fund domestic loans. As US banks with foreign branches showed a better ability to weather the credit crunches of 1966 and 1969-70, more and more US banks made the decision to become an MNE by opening either a London or a Nassau (Bahamas) branch. Even after the elimination of Regulation Q for large time deposits in 1970, access to the Eurodollar market — and to foreign deposits — remained a powerful reason for US banks to become MNEs.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">美国银行跨国企业的崛起与欧洲美元市场的诞生和发展相伴而生。这使得美国银行得以在海外筹集美元，不受 Q 条例（一项限制美国银行国内分支机构定期存款利率的法规）的约束。由于该条例不适用于海外，许多美国银行利用欧洲美元市场筹集资金，再将这些资金转贷给国内分支机构，以支持国内贷款。随着拥有海外分行的美国银行展现出了更强的抵御 1966 年和 1969-70 年信贷紧缩的能力，越来越多的美国银行决定通过在伦敦或拿骚（巴哈马）开设分行来成为跨国企业。即使在 1970 年大额定期存款的 Q 条例被废除后，进入欧洲美元市场以及获取海外存款仍然是美国银行成为跨国企业的重要动因。</span></span></span>

A smaller number of US banks became MNEs in order to participate in the emerging global capital market as lenders as well as deposit-takers or borrowers. Such banks included Security Pacific, a large California-based banking organization, which established a branch in London in 1969. Security Pacific subsequently expanded into securities origination and distribution, buying Hoare Govett in the UK and Burns, Fry in Canada when these two countries liberalized their domestic regulatory regimes to permit banks to become affiliated with securities brokers and dealers. This capital markets focus was also shared by longer-established players, such as Morgan Guaranty, who expanded their branch network from London to other financial centres in continental Europe and in the Far East.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">少数美国银行为参与新兴全球资本市场，既作为贷款人又作为存款接收方或借款方而成为跨国企业。这类银行包括总部位于加州的大型银行机构 Security Pacific，它于 1969 年在伦敦设立分行。随后 Security Pacific 拓展至证券发行与分销业务，趁英国和加拿大放宽国内监管制度、允许银行与证券经纪商及交易商建立关联之际，收购了英国的 Hoare Govett 和加拿大的 Burns, Fry。这种聚焦资本市场的策略也得到摩根担保等老牌机构的追随，它们将分行网络从伦敦扩展至欧洲大陆及远东的其他金融中心。</span></span></span>

Finally, a handful of US bank MNEs attempted to compete in local banking markets around the world. Foremost among these was Citicorp/Citibank. In 1960 the bank had 70 foreign branches; at year-end 1988 the company had 2,135 offices in 89 countries <span id="Ch013.xhtml_page_255"></span>outside the United States.<sup>14</sup> The expansion had four aspects. The first was to broaden the network's coverage; the second was to deepen the network in each major country; the third was to broaden the customer base by serving consumers; and the fourth was to broaden the product mix from banking to financial services.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">最终，少数几家美国银行跨国企业试图在全球各地的本土金融市场一争高下。其中首屈一指的当属花旗集团（Citicorp/Citibank）。1960 年，该行仅有 70 家海外分支机构；而到 1988 年底，已在海外 89 个国家拥有 2135 个办事处。 <sup>14</sup> 其扩张体现在四个方面：一是拓宽网点覆盖范围；二是深耕每个主要国家的市场网络；三是通过服务个人消费者来扩大客户群体；四是将业务组合从传统银行扩展到金融服务领域。</span></span></span>

During the 1960s the emphasis was primarily on broadenmg and deepening the network's coverage, so that it became a truly global network. During the 1970s and 1980s the emphasis was on broadening the customer base and expanding the range of products. In particular, the Citicorp formed a global consumer banking group in 1974 to guide its thrust into the individual marketplace around the world. By year end 1988 it served over eight million households in 40 different countries outside the US.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">在 20 世纪 60 年代，重点主要在于扩大和深化网络覆盖，使其成为一个真正的全球网络。到了 70 年代和 80 年代，重点转向拓宽客户基础和扩大产品范围。尤其是，花旗集团（Citicorp）于 1974 年成立了全球消费者银行集团，以推动其向全球个人市场的进军。截至 1988 年底，它为美国以外 40 个不同国家的超过 800 万户家庭提供服务。</span></span></span>

As the customer base expanded, so too did the range of financial services offered to both individual and institutional customers. In line with banking practice in most countries outside the US, Citicorp offered securities services, including underwriting and distribution, principally to institutional clients. In the 1980s Citicorp also moved into insurance, buying a Lloyds insurance broker and establishing or acquiring life insurance companies in a number of other countries.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">随着客户基础的扩大，提供给个人和机构客户的金融服务范围也相应扩展。与大多数美国以外国家的银行业惯例一致，花旗集团主要向机构客户提供证券服务，包括承销和分销。在 80 年代，花旗集团还涉足保险业，收购了一家劳合社（Lloyds）保险经纪公司，并在多个其他国家设立或收购了人寿保险公司。</span></span></span>

Taken together, this geographic, customer and product diversification gave substance to the company's view of itself as a global financial services corporation. During the late 1970s well over half of the corporation's total revenues and pre-tax earnings came from outside the United States. Although this proportion has recently fallen back to below 50 per cent as a result of the company's strong growth in the US, Citicorp 'far exceeds' other US bank MNEs in terms of the extent of its network and the scope of activities in which it engages outside the United States.<sup>15</sup> In fact, in most countries outside the United States, Citicorp is the largest foreign banking organization, and in some emerging economies it has a market share equal to or exceeding its market share in the US.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">总体来看，这种地理、客户和产品的多元化，夯实了公司作为全球金融服务企业的自我认知。20 世纪 70 年代末，公司总收入及税前利润中超过一半来自美国境外。尽管近年来因花旗在美国本土的强劲增长，这一比例回落至 50%以下，但花旗银行（Citicorp）在美国境外的网络覆盖范围和业务活动广度“远超”其他美国银行跨国企业。 <sup>15</sup> 事实上，在美国以外的大多数国家，花旗都是最大的外国银行机构；在一些新兴经济体中，其市场份额甚至达到或超过了在美国本土的水平。</span></span></span>

On the whole, Citicorp's expansion into foreign markets took the form of wholly-owned branches or subsidiaries. Few joint ventures were tried. Nor did the company take significant minority positions in many foreign banks. TTie clear preference was for complete control, and the exceptions to that rule tended to end in failure (from Citicorp's point of view).<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">总体来看，花旗银行在海外扩张时，主要采取全资分行或全资子公司的形式，极少尝试合资模式，也很少持有外国银行的少数股权。花旗显然更倾向于完全控制，而那些违背这一原则的例外情况，最终往往以失败告终（从花旗的角度来看）。</span></span></span>

In contrast, Chase followed a policy of seeking local partners for expansion in key markets. In 1960 it had a network of 24 branches in 11 different foreign countries, compared to 70 for Citibank, spread among well over 20 different countries. To catch up, Chase embarked upon a series of joint ventures with local banks in Latin <span id="Ch013.xhtml_page_256"></span>America, Europe, Australia and Africa. Such ventures created more problems than profits. In South America, Chase made investments in local banks in Brazil, Argentina, Venezuela, Peru, Colombia and Honduras. Only the Brazilian investment turned out well; the others produced losses. In Europe, Chase ran into similar problems with its investments in local banks in Austria, Belgium, Ireland and the Netherlands. Nor was its investment in Australia without problems. In Africa, Chase concluded an agreement with the Chartered Bank whereby Chase contributed its African branches to Chartered in return for a minority stake in Chartered. That agreement dissolved when Chartered merged with the Standard Bank, diluting Chase's share. Chase in effect lost control over the branches that it had placed into the joint venture, and it had to rebuild its African network from scratch during the 1970s.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">相比之下，大通银行采取了与当地合作伙伴共同拓展关键市场的策略。1960 年，它在 11 个不同国家拥有 24 家分行，而花旗银行在 20 多个国家设有 70 家分行。为追赶差距，大通在拉丁美洲、欧洲、澳大利亚和非洲与当地银行展开了一系列合资合作。然而，这些合作带来的问题远多于利润。在南美，大通在巴西、阿根廷、委内瑞拉、秘鲁、哥伦比亚和洪都拉斯等地投资了当地银行，但除了巴西的投资还算成功外，其余均以亏损告终。在欧洲，大通在奥地利、比利时、爱尔兰和荷兰的银行投资也遭遇了类似困境。其在澳大利亚的投资同样波折不断。在非洲，大通与渣打银行达成协议，将自己在非洲的分行注入渣打，以换取渣打银行的少数股权。 该协议随着 Chartered 与 Standard Bank 合并而终止，Chase 的股权被稀释。Chase 实际上失去了对原本投入合资企业的分支机构的控制权，因此在 20 世纪 70 年代不得不从零开始重建其在非洲的业务网络。</span></span></span>

Chase's direct efforts were more successful, In the Caribbean (especially Puerto Rico) it expanded aggressively via <span class="italic">de novo </span>expansion. In Asia, it acquired branches in Bangkok, Singapore and Hong Kong from a Dutch bank, the Nationale Handelsbank and it opened <span class="italic">de novo</span> branches in Malaysia, Korea, the Philippines, Indonesia, Vietnam and Taiwan. In Europe it opened branches in Italy and Greece.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">Chase 的直接扩张行动更为成功。在加勒比地区（尤其是波多黎各），它通过新设机构积极扩张。在亚洲，它从一家荷兰银行 Nationale Handelsbank 手中收购了曼谷、新加坡和香港的分行，并在马来西亚、韩国、菲律宾、印度尼西亚、越南和台湾开设新分行。在欧洲，它在意大利和希腊设立了分行。</span></span></span>

By 1970 Chase had operations in 73 different countries, either through wholly-owned branches (25 countries) or partially-owned affiliates. This gave Chase a global network to serve its major US corporate customers, who themselves were becoming steadily more multinational. During the 1970s and 1980s Chase concentrated on establishing full control over its foreign operations and on broadening the product range offered by its branches and affiliates overseas. By 1985 Chase had wholly-owned branches in 45 countries, and it conducted a full range of securities activities as well as deposit and credit activities.<sup>16</sup><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">到 1970 年，Chase 已在 73 个国家开展业务，其中 25 个国家设有全资分行，其余为部分持股的附属机构。这为 Chase 构建了全球网络，服务于其日益跨国化的美国大型企业客户。在 20 世纪 70 至 80 年代，Chase 专注于掌控其海外业务的完全控制权，并拓展海外分行及附属机构的产品范围。到 1985 年，Chase 在 45 个国家拥有全资分行，并全面开展证券业务以及存贷款业务。 <sup>16</sup> </span></span></span>

Since 1985 many US bank MNEs, including Chase, have undertaken to rationalize their foreign operations. With the exception of Citicorp, practically all US bank MNEs have pulled out of individual banking outside the US. In 1989, for example, Chase announced the sale of its Belgian subsidiary to Credit Lyonnais. Earlier, Chemical Banking Corporation had sold its private bank in Switzerland and reduced its operations elsewhere in Europe. Wells Fargo cut back substantially on its foreign branch operations and reached a joint marketing agreement with the Hongkong and Shanghai Bank whereby each would market the other's products in their respective home areas. Bank of America also curtailed its overseas activities, selling its Italian bank subsidiary, Banca d'America e d'ltalia, to the Deutsche Bank in 1986 in order to <span id="Ch013.xhtml_page_257"></span>raise capital. In addition, some US bank MNEs have merged with one another or with a foreign banking organization, and they have consolidated their foreign operations into a single entity.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">自 1985 年以来，包括大通银行在内的许多美国银行跨国企业开始对其海外业务进行合理化调整。除花旗银行外，几乎所有美国银行跨国企业都退出了美国以外的个人银行业务。例如，1989 年大通银行宣布将其比利时子公司出售给法国里昂信贷银行。更早之前，化工银行将其在瑞士的私人银行出售，并缩减了在欧洲其他地区的业务。富国银行大幅削减了其海外分行业务，并与汇丰银行达成联合营销协议，双方在各自的本土市场推广对方的产品。美国银行也缩减了海外活动，于 1986 年将其意大利银行子公司 Banca d'America e d'ltalia 出售给德意志银行以筹集资金。 此外，一些美国银行 MNE 相互合并，或与外国银行组织合并，并将其海外业务整合为一个单一实体。</span></span></span>

Despite this recent decline, the growth in the number and foreign assets of US banks' MNEs over the past thirty years has been dramatic, particularly among the largest institutions. In 1960, over two-thirds of the largest 25 US banks were not MNEs; by 1978 every single one of the top 25 was an MNE. And, at the end of 1987, each of those top 25 banks (with the exception of Wells Fargo) had foreign assets well in excess of 10 per cent of the bank's total, with the average for the group as a whole being over 40 per cent.<sup>17</sup><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">尽管近期有所下滑，但过去三十年间，美国银行 MNE 的数量和海外资产增长惊人，尤以规模最大的几家机构为甚。1960 年，美国 25 家最大银行中超过三分之二还不是 MNE；而到 1978 年，这 25 家银行无一例外全都成为了 MNE。此外，截至 1987 年底，这 25 家银行中（富国银行除外）每家银行的海外资产都远超其总资产的 10%，该群体整体的平均比例甚至超过了 40%。 <sup>17</sup> </span></span></span>

### Non-US bank MNEs in the US<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">在美国的非美国银行 MNE</span></span></span>

Parallel to the rise of US bank MNEs was an expansion of non-US bank MNEs into the US. During the period 1972 to 1987 the share of non-US bank MNEs in total domestic banking assets rose from 4 to 18 per cent — a fourfold increase (see [Figure 13.1](#Ch013.xhtml_cap_lbl0002)). Total assets of non-US bank MNEs in the US amounted to nearly \$600 billion at the end of 1987. The number of non-US bank MNEs with US offices rose from 79 in 1975 to 246 at the end of 1987, while the total number of US branches and agencies of such banks rose during the same period from 147 to 514.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">与美国银行跨国公司崛起同步的是，非美国银行跨国公司向美国市场的扩张。1972 年至 1987 年间，非美国银行跨国公司在美国国内银行总资产中的占比从 4%上升至 18%，增长了三倍（见图 13.1）。截至 1987 年底，非美国银行跨国公司在美总资产已接近 6000 亿美元。在美设有分支机构的非美国银行跨国公司数量从 1975 年的 79 家增至 1987 年底的 246 家，同期这些银行在美国的分行和代理行总数也从 147 家增至 514 家。</span></span></span>

These non-US banks came from at least thirty different countries around the world. In terms of numbers of branches and total assets, banks from Japan were the largest single national contingent, accounting for approximately 15 per cent of the total number of branches and over half of the total assets of all US branches and agencies of foreign banks. Other countries with large contingents in terms of numbers of banks, branches and/or assets included the UK, Germany, Italy and France. Taken together, the Member States of the European Community accounted for a larger number of banks and branches than did Japan.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">这些非美国银行来自全球至少三十个国家。就分行数量和总资产而言，日本银行是规模最大的单一国家群体，其分行数量约占外国银行在美国所有分行及代理机构总数的 15%，而资产总额则超过一半。其他在银行数量、分行数量或资产规模上颇为可观的国家包括英国、德国、意大利和法国。综合来看，欧共体成员国所拥有的银行和分行数量甚至超过了日本。</span></span></span>

From a strategic point of view, non-US bank MNEs can be divided into roughly two camps: those that plan to serve a broad range of customer groups; and those that plan to concentrate on large corporate customers. The first group tends to own a subsidiary bank within the US as well as operate through a branch. The subsidiary bank generally serves the individual and small-to-medium sized corporate market, and is frequently used by the non-US bank MNE as a vehicle for further expansion within the US. The second group tends not to have a subsidiary bank in the US, but to operate through a branch of the parent bank. The primary<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">从战略角度来看，非美国银行跨国公司大致可分为两个阵营：一类计划服务广泛的客户群体，另一类则专注于大型企业客户。前者通常在美国境内设立子银行，同时通过分行运营。子银行主要面向个人和中小型企业市场，常被非美国银行跨国公司用作其在美国进一步扩张的载体。后者通常不在美国设立子银行，而是通过母行的分行开展业务。</span></span></span>

A bank with a broad approach which clearly belongs in the first group is the Hongkong and Shanghai Banking Corporation, the largest non-US bank MNE in the US at the end of 1987, with \$21.8 billion in domestic banking assets in its branches, agencies and wholly-owned subsidiaries. In 1979 it had purchased a controlling interest in Marine Midland Bank Corp., at a time when that bank was struggling to put its loan portfolio in order. Since then, Hongkong has acquired complete control of Marine Midland and used it as a vehicle to expand within the US. Marine Midland itself has returned to health, and it has begun to acquire banks in other states along the East Coast. More recently, Hongkong and Shanghai announced plans for a cross marketing agreement with Wells Fargo, a California-based US bank MNE that is contracting its foreign activities. At the same time Hongkong has kept its New York branch in operation.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">一家业务范围广泛、显然属于第一类的银行是汇丰银行（Hongkong and Shanghai Banking Corporation）。截至 1987 年底，它是美国最大的非美国银行跨国企业，其分行、代理行及全资子公司的国内银行资产总额达 218 亿美元。1979 年，它收购了 Marine Midland Bank Corp.的控股权，而当时这家银行正艰难地调整其贷款组合。此后，汇丰完全掌控了 Marine Midland，并将其作为在美国扩张的跳板。Marine Midland 自身也恢复了健康，并开始收购东海岸其他州的银行。最近，汇丰宣布与总部位于加州、正在收缩海外业务的美国银行跨国企业 Wells Fargo 达成一项交叉营销协议。与此同时，汇丰仍保留着其纽约分行的运营。</span></span></span>

A number of other non-US bank MNEs are also following a similar strategy of serving large wholesale customers through the branches and agencies of the parent bank, while expanding into the individual and middle corporate market in the US through the vehicle of wholly-owned subsidiary US banks. Perhaps most prominent among them is National Westminster Bank, the large UK clearing bank. NatWest acquired the National Bank of North America, changed its name to NatWest USA and has since used that vehicle to expand into New Jersey. Its ambition is to continue to expand in the Middle Atlantic States, with a focus on banking for individuals and small-to-medium sized businesses — exactly the same target groups that its UK high street branches serve so successfully. Other non-US bank MNEs taking this route include the Bank of Montreal (Harris Bank Corp. in Chicago); ABN (La Salle National Bank and Exchange National Bank, both in Chicago); Sanwa Bank (Sanwa Bank California) and Allied Irish Banks (First National Maryland).<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\

In contrast, there are a fair number of non-US bank MNEs that have decided to curtail such a strategy in favour of a more focused approach to the large corporate marketplace. These banks have in some cases sold off their US bank subsidiaries, either to other non-US bank MNEs or to US banks. All are now focusing on institutional banking for major corporate customers.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\

### Summary<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">摘要</span></span></span>

Since 1960 growth in US multinational banking has been dramatic, both by US bank MNEs outside the US and by non-US bank MNEs in the US. This has brought about a vast change in the US marketplace. In 1960 one could reasonably conclude that the US market was cut off from the rest of the world. Only eight US banks had offices outside the US, and few non-US banks had offices in the US. Events in world markets had little impact on financing for US issuers or on the opportunities available to US investors.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">自 1960 年以来，US 跨国银行业务增长迅猛，无论是 US 银行 MNEs 在海外，还是非 US 银行 MNEs 在 US，都呈现爆发式扩张。这彻底改变了 US 市场格局。1960 年时，完全有理由认为 US 市场与世界其他地区是隔绝的。当时仅有八家 US 银行在海外设有分支机构，而几乎没有非 US 银行在 US 开展业务。全球市场动向对 US 发行人的融资能力以及 US 投资者的投资机遇几乎毫无影响。</span></span></span>

That is no longer the case. Multinational banking is now the norm. The majority of US banking assets are controlled either by US banks with foreign offices or by non-US bank MNEs operating in the US (see [Table 13.2](#Ch013.xhtml_fig0025)). Since these MNEs compete with each other in the global marketplace, and since there are no capital or exchange controls in the US, prices for financial services in the US tend to be determined in the world marketplace, rather than just the domestic market. This tends to put downward pressure on prices for financial services and to raise the quality and diversity of services available.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">如今情况已非如此。跨国银行业务已成为常态。美国大部分银行资产要么由设有海外办事处的美国银行控制，要么由在美国运营的非美国银行跨国企业掌控（见表 13.2）。由于这些跨国企业在全球市场上相互竞争，且美国没有资本管制或外汇管制，美国金融服务的价格往往由全球市场而非仅由国内市场决定。这通常会对金融服务价格形成下行压力，同时提升服务的质量与多样性。</span></span></span>

Reasons for the rise of banks as multinationals<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">银行崛起为跨国企业的原因</span></span></span>
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

What accounts for the almost complete absence of US multinational banking during the period before 1960, and the surge since? What accounts for the 'two-way' flow of multinational banking (US bank MNEs abroad and non-US bank MNEs into the US)?<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">是什么原因导致 1960 年之前美国几乎完全没有跨国银行业务，而此后却迅猛增长？又是什么造成了跨国银行业务的“双向”流动（美国银行跨国企业走向海外，非美国银行跨国企业进入美国）？</span></span></span>

The reasons can be grouped under three headings: macroeconomic, regulatory and microeconomic. In each of these areas the era before 1960 contrasts sharply with that after it, and it does so in ways that favoured the formation of bank MNEs.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">这些原因可归纳为三大类：宏观经济、监管政策和微观经济。在这三个领域中，1960 年前后的时代形成了鲜明对比，且这种差异恰好有利于银行跨国公司的形成。</span></span></span>

Broadly speaking, the macroeconomic and political environment prior to 1960 was not conducive to foreign investment in general, and perhaps to banking investments in particular. War and depression are not the best background in which to conduct an international banking business, and for much of the period 1914 to 1960 the world was either at war or in an economic depression. <span id="Ch013.xhtml_page_262"></span>Only during the 1950s, when the postwar recovery was well underway and when the threat of war had receded did the climate for international investment improve.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">从宏观角度看，1960 年之前的政治经济环境整体上不利于外国投资，尤其是银行业投资。战争与经济萧条绝非开展国际银行业务的理想背景，而在 1914 年至 1960 年的大部分时间里，世界不是处于战争就是陷入经济萧条。直到 20 世纪 50 年代，战后重建步入正轨，战争威胁逐渐消退，国际投资环境才得以改善。</span></span></span>

Since 1960, that climate has remained a favourable one. Compared to the decades prior to 1960, the decades after have been ones of relatively stable real economic growth, and relatively stable political regimes. In addition, exchange controls have been relaxed, trade barriers reduced and foreign investment promoted. In 1958 European currencies were made convertible, and since then there has been a progressive decline in the number of countries imposing exchange controls, the range of transactions that such controls cover, and the severity of the controls that remain in place. All this augured well for an explosion in multinational banking.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">自 1960 年以来，这种环境一直保持着有利态势。与 1960 年之前的几十年相比，之后的几十年里实际经济增长相对稳定，政治体制也相对稳固。此外，外汇管制逐步放宽，贸易壁垒不断降低，外国投资得到促进。1958 年欧洲货币实现可兑换，此后实施外汇管制的国家数量、此类管制所涵盖的交易范围以及仍存续管制的严格程度均呈下降趋势。所有这些都为跨国银行业的蓬勃发展创造了良好前景。</span></span></span>

So did changes in regulation. Since I960, countries around the world have opened their markets to entry by foreign banking organizations and removed barriers to expansion by domestic banking organizations into foreign markets. These regulatory changes have facilitated the growth of US bank MNEs and of non-US bank MNEs in the US. For example, since 1960 Spain, Canada, Australia and Sweden have removed prohibitions on entry by foreign banks, and US bank MNEs have figured prominently among the new entrants into these markets.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">监管方面的变化也是如此。自 1960 年以来，世界各国纷纷向外国银行机构开放市场，并消除了国内银行机构向海外市场扩张的障碍。这些监管变化促进了美国银行跨国企业以及在美国的非美国银行跨国企业的发展。例如，自 1960 年以来，西班牙、加拿大、澳大利亚和瑞典已取消对外资银行进入的禁令，而美国银行跨国企业在这些市场的进入者中占据了显著地位。</span></span></span>

The US has also facilitated the entry of foreign banking organizations into its market. In 1978 it passed the International Banking Act, which put foreign banking organizations on a par with their US counterparts — a policy known as national treatment. This assured that branches and subsidiaries of foreign banks would be treated in the US as if they were US banks. Therefore, as the barriers to interstate banking in the US fell, foreign banking organizations stood to benefit from these changes as well. This made the US market more attractive to foreign banking organizations, and may have been a factor in encouraging foreign banking organizations to make acquisitions of US institutions.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">美国也为外国银行机构进入其市场提供了便利。1978 年，美国通过了《国际银行法》，将外国银行机构置于与美国同行同等的地位——这一政策被称为国民待遇。该法案确保外国银行的分行和子公司在美国将受到与美国银行相同的待遇。因此，随着美国跨州银行业务壁垒的消除，外国银行机构同样从这些变化中受益。这使得美国市场对外国银行机构更具吸引力，并可能成为鼓励外国银行机构收购美国机构的一个因素。</span></span></span>

Restrictions on the domestic activities of banking organizations also played a role in fostering banks as multinationals, both in terms of pushing US banks out into the world and in terms of pulling foreign banks into the US. The clearest case of the former was Regulation Q, which, as mentioned above, fostered the Eurodollar market and induced scores of US banking organizations to set up foreign offices solely for the purpose of tapping this source of funds. As for the latter, limits on access to the dollar payments system to entities domiciled in the US has undoubtedly helped induce many foreign banking organizations to establish branches <span id="Ch013.xhtml_page_263"></span>or acquire subsidiaries in the US, especially in New York.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">对银行组织国内活动的限制，在推动美国银行走向世界以及吸引外国银行进入美国方面，也对银行跨国化起到了作用。最典型的例子是 Regulation Q，如上所述，它催生了欧洲美元市场，并促使众多美国银行组织仅为了获取这一资金来源而设立海外办事处。至于后者，对非美国本土实体接入美元支付系统的限制，无疑促使许多外国银行组织在美国（尤其是纽约）设立分行或收购子公司。</span></span></span>

But all these changes probably would not have led to the explosion in multinational banking that has occurred, were it not for 'microeconomic' change — the transformation that has taken place in the business of banking itself. Prior to 1960, market relationships could compete quite well with intrafirm transactions. The correspondent banking system worked extremely well for the simple payments transactions and trade finance that were the staple of international banking. There was little edge to be had via internalization; consequently, there were few multinational banks. After 1960, the story was different. Multinational banks did have an edge.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">然而，若非“微观经济”层面的变革——即银行业务本身的转型——所有这些变化或许不会引发跨国银行行业的爆发式增长。1960 年以前，市场关系在内部交易面前完全能够与之竞争。代理行体系在处理简单的支付交易和贸易融资方面运作得极为高效，而这些正是国际银行业的核心业务。通过内部化几乎无利可图；因此，跨国银行寥寥无几。1960 年之后，情况截然不同。跨国银行确实占据了优势。</span></span></span>

Three fundamental forces brought about this trend toward globalization in banking. The first of these was technology. Since 1964, the real cost of recording, transmitting and processing information (including financial information) has fallen more than 95 per cent. This tremendous cost reduction makes it cheaper to assess the risks and rewards of financial assets, cheaper to record and process trades, cheaper to manage portfolios and cheaper to match the users with the suppliers of capital.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">推动银行业全球化趋势的三大基本力量中，首当其冲的是技术。自 1964 年以来，记录、传输和处理信息（包括金融信息）的实际成本已下降了 95%以上。这一巨大的成本降低，使得评估金融资产的风险与回报变得更加便宜，记录和处理交易更加便宜，管理投资组合更加便宜，以及匹配资本供需双方更加便宜。</span></span></span>

The second major force at work was the institutionalization, or changing pattern, of household savings. Throughout the world, the individual investor gave way to the institutional investor. This should not be surprising. Few individual investors have the resources, ability or time to manage their own portfolios as well as an institution can do it for them. Institutional forms of saving, such as pension funds, often have tax advantages not available to individuals. Institutions also offer better diversification than individuals can hope to achieve on their own. Relative to each dollar invested, institutions can manage portfolios at lower cost than can individuals. Finally, fiduciary obligations and competitive pressures induce institutions to provide professional, prudent management and best execution for their customers. Consequently, individuals tend to concentrate their efforts on earning and saving; they let institutions do the investing.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">第二个重要力量是家庭储蓄的机构化，或者说储蓄模式的转变。在全球范围内，个人投资者逐渐让位于机构投资者。这并不令人意外。很少有个人投资者拥有足够的资源、能力或时间来像机构那样管理好自己的投资组合。像养老金基金这样的机构储蓄形式，通常享有个体无法获得的税收优惠。机构还能提供比个人自行投资更优的分散化配置。相对于每投入的一美元，机构管理投资组合的成本也比个人更低。最后，信托义务和竞争压力促使机构为客户提供专业、审慎的管理和最佳执行。因此，个人更倾向于专注于赚钱和储蓄，而把投资的事情交给机构去做。</span></span></span>

The third major force behind globalization was financial innovation. All of this professionally managed capital would mean very little, if there weren't a wide range of choices for investment. Advances in the theory of finance, combined with technology, have made it possible to develop a wide range of new derivative financial instruments, such as options, swaps and futures, and to trade these new derivatives. By using derivatives, it is possible to segregate exchange rate risk, credit risk, interest rate risk, and the liquidity risk that used to be bundled together, for better or for <span id="Ch013.xhtml_page_264"></span>worse, in the traditional loan or security. In particular, by using currency swaps, it is possible too for an investor to lay off the foreign exchange risk in a foreign bond or stock. This makes it easier for investors to analyse and manage global portfolios.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">推动全球化的第三大力量是金融创新。如果没有多样化的投资选择，这些由专业管理的资本将意义甚微。金融理论的发展与技术进步相结合，催生了一系列新型衍生金融工具，如期权、掉期和期货，并使得这些新衍生品的交易成为可能。通过使用衍生工具，可以将过去在传统贷款或证券中捆绑在一起的汇率风险、信用风险、利率风险和流动性风险进行分离。特别是，通过货币掉期，投资者可以对冲外国债券或股票的外汇风险。这使投资者能够更轻松地分析和管理全球投资组合。</span></span></span>

Taken together, these three forces have produced what ought be called a new finance. From a world of commercial banking, investment banking and insurance segmented into national markets, banks are moving towards a world of financial engineering, financial origination/distribution and financial administration — a three-dimensional finance conducted in a single, global marketplace in which there is a premium on being able to co-ordinate within the firm across markets, cultures and time zones. Firms that wish to be major players in finance therefore have to be multinationals, and no multinational financial firm can afford to be absent from the biggest financial market of all, the United States.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">综上所述，这三种力量共同催生了一种应被称为“新金融”的形态。银行正从依托国家市场分割的商业银行、投资银行和保险业，迈向一个由金融工程、金融发起/分销与金融管理构成的三维金融世界——这一模式在统一全球市场中运作，核心能力在于能够跨市场、跨文化、跨时区在企业内部实现协调。因此，任何希望成为金融领域主要参与者的公司都必须成为跨国公司，而没有任何一家跨国金融公司能够承受缺席全球最大金融市场——美国——的代价。</span></span></span>

The influence of the MNE’s home country<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">跨国企业（MNE）母国的影响力</span></span></span>
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

How particular financial institutions fare in this new global environment depends to a large extent on the financial institution's 'home' country. Home countries regulate the types of activities in which a company may engage, the corporate structure it must have, the capital it must keep, the taxes it must pay, and the accounting it must follow. Although subsidiaries and branches in host countries follow the practices of the host country, it is ultimately the home country that is most influential, and differences in home country regulation can lead to quite large advantages or disadvantages in the marketplace.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">各类金融机构在这个全新全球环境中的表现，很大程度上取决于其母国。母国规范了企业可从事的业务类型、必须采用的公司架构、需持有的资本、应缴纳的税款以及须遵循的会计准则。尽管东道国的子公司和分支机构需遵循当地惯例，但最终最具影响力的仍是母国，而母国监管的差异可能导致企业在市场中占据截然不同的优势或劣势。</span></span></span>

How do US bank MNEs compare with bank MNEs with different home countries? The record seems clear. US bank MNEs are losing market share relative to bank MNEs with other home countries. On a global basis (home and foreign activities combined), US bank MNEs ranked among the largest in the world ten and fifteen years ago; today they are much lower on any list of world rankings. More significantly, perhaps, the foreign assets of US bank MNEs now rank behind the foreign assets of bank MNEs from other countries. Indeed, the assets of non-US bank MNEs in the US alone now exceed the total foreign assets of US bank MNEs.<sup>18</sup><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">美国银行跨国企业与其他国家的银行跨国企业相比如何？答案似乎很清楚。美国银行跨国企业相对于其他国家的银行跨国企业而言，正在失去市场份额。从全球范围（国内与国外业务合计）来看，十年前和十五年前，美国银行跨国企业还位居世界最大企业之列；而如今，它们在各种世界排名榜单上的位置都已大幅下滑。或许更重要的是，美国银行跨国企业的海外资产现在已落后于其他国家的银行跨国企业的海外资产。事实上，仅在美国境内的非美国银行跨国企业的资产，就已超过美国银行跨国企业的全部海外资产。 <sup>18</sup> </span></span></span>

In part, this relative rise of non-US bank MNEs may be a result of the bank being in the right place at the right time. For example, Japanese bank MNEs naturally do a large business in Japan, one of the largest and most rapidly growing economies in the world. As the Japanese economy grows, so will Japanese banks, and they <span id="Ch013.xhtml_page_265"></span>may chose to reinvest some portion of their earnings to enter other markets, including the United States. The extraordinarily high price/earnings multiples accorded all stocks in Japan (and the consequent low cost of capital) accrues to Japanese banks as well.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">非美国银行跨国公司相对崛起的部分原因，可能在于它们恰逢其时、恰逢其地。例如，日本银行跨国公司自然在日本这一全球规模最大、增长最快的经济体之一中开展大量业务。随着日本经济增长，日本银行也将同步壮大，它们可能会选择将部分收益再投资，以进入包括美国在内的其他市场。日本股市全体股票所享有的超高市盈率（及由此带来的低资本成本），同样惠及日本银行。</span></span></span>

In part, however, the relative decline of US bank MNEs may be due to the disadvantages of having the US as a home country. In particular, the US regulatory system is not well adapted to the needs of the new finance. The separation of commercial and investment banking mandated by the Glass Steagall Act is increasingly outmoded in an environment where neither issuers nor investors care about the distinction. In general, US banking organizations have fewer powers, appear to have less favourable accounting and tax treatment, must employ a clumsier holding company corporate structure, and may face heavier <span class="italic">de facto</span> capital requirements than do bank MNEs with other home countries. Relatively speaking, US bank MNEs also have less opportunity to expand within their home markets. There are barriers to nationwide branching, prohibitions on engaging in securities and insurance activities, and limitations on affiliation with or investments in non-financial enterprises. These disadvantages do not appear to be offset by a greater degree of official support for US bank MNEs by the central bank than that accorded non-US bank MNEs by their respective home countries. All this bodes ill for the future of US bank MNEs, but may bode well for the future of non-US bank MNEs in the United States.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">然而，美国银行跨国公司的相对衰落，部分原因可能在于其母国美国的劣势。特别是，美国的监管体系未能很好地适应新金融的需求。《格拉斯-斯蒂格尔法案》强制要求分离商业银行与投资银行业务，而在发行方和投资者都不再在意这一区别的环境下，这种做法已日益不合时宜。总体而言，与其他母国的银行跨国公司相比，美国银行组织的权限更少，会计和税收待遇似乎更为不利，必须采用更笨拙的控股公司架构，并可能面临更严格的实际资本要求。相对而言，美国银行跨国公司在本国市场扩张的机会也更少：存在全国性分支机构的设立障碍，禁止从事证券和保险业务，并且对与非金融企业的关联或投资设有限制。 这些劣势似乎并未因美国央行对美国银行跨国企业的官方支持力度大于非美国银行跨国企业母国对其的支持而得到弥补。所有这些迹象对美国银行跨国企业的未来而言并非吉兆，但对于非美国银行跨国企业在美国的前景却可能是个好消息。</span></span></span>

Nor can these differences be eliminated by a policy of national treatment. They are at the heart of the home country regulation of the parent organization, and so pervade the global operations of the MNE. All national treatment can do is assure that the host country does not treat foreign bank MNEs worse than it treats its own institutions. National treatment does not prevent home countries from exercising what amounts to reverse discrimination against institutions for which they regulate the ultimate parent. And, in a global marketplace, institutions that suffer such reverse discrimination must ultimately lose market share.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">而这些差异也无法通过国民待遇政策消除。它们根植于母国对母公司的监管方式，从而渗透到跨国企业的全球运营中。国民待遇所能做的只是确保东道国对外国银行跨国企业的待遇不会比本国机构更差。国民待遇并不能阻止母国对由其监管最终母公司的机构实施事实上的反向歧视。而在全球市场中，遭受这种反向歧视的机构最终必将失去市场份额。</span></span></span>

Conclusion<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">结论</span></span></span>
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

In sum, US multinational banking is a relatively recent phenomenon. Before 1960, few US banks were multinationals, and few non-US bank MNEs operated in the US. Today, the opposite is true. Ail major US banks are MNEs; and all major non-US bank MNEs conduct significant operations in the US. Indeed, the bulk of US banking activity is now carried on by MNEs.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">总而言之，美国跨国银行业是个相对较新的现象。1960 年之前，几乎没有几家美国银行是跨国企业，同时也很少有非美国银行跨国公司在美经营。如今情况恰恰相反。所有美国大型银行都已发展为跨国企业；而所有主要的非美国银行跨国公司也在美国开展大规模业务。事实上，如今美国银行业的大部分活动都由跨国公司主导。</span></span></span>

<span id="Ch013.xhtml_page_266"></span>This surge in MNEs ultimately stems from changes in the business of banking itself. Advances in technology have lowered the cost, and the advent of financial engineering has raised the reward of conducting transactions 'in-house' rather than in the market via correspondents. As a result, all major banks are now MNEs. In such a global banking market, home country regulation will play a large role in determining the competitive position of individual firms. US regulation of US bank MNEs places such firms at a disadvantage, and it is anticipated that, barring a significant change in US regulation, US bank MNEs will continue to lose market share in the global banking market, and that non-US bank MNEs will continue to gain market share in the US.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">跨国公司的激增最终源于银行业务本身的变化。技术进步降低了成本，金融工程的出现提高了通过内部交易而非通过代理行在市场上进行交易的回报。因此，所有大型银行现在都是跨国公司。在这样的全球银行市场中，母国监管将在决定个体公司的竞争地位方面发挥重要作用。美国对美国银行跨国公司的监管使这些公司处于不利地位，预计除非美国监管发生重大变化，否则美国银行跨国公司将继续在全球银行市场中失去市场份额，而非美国银行跨国公司将继续在美国获得市场份额。</span></span></span>
