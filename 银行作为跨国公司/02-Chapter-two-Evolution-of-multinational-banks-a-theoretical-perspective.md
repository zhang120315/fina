Introduction<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">引言</span></span></span>
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

This chapter presents a theoretical framework for analysing the evolution of multinational banking. The first part (pp. 14-20) considers whether the economic motives for the multinationalization of banking are the same as those for manufacturing. This involves a straightforward application of conventional economic theory, based on the internalization approach. The second part (pp. 20-7) attempts to explain the dominance of certain groups of people and certain nations at various stages in the history of multinational banking. To tackle this broader issue, it is necessary to embed conventional theory within a more general framework which includes social and political, as well as economic perspectives.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">本章提出了一个用于分析跨国银行业演进的理论框架。第一部分（第 14-20 页）探讨了银行业跨国化的经济动机是否与制造业相同。这涉及基于内部化方法的传统经济理论的直接应用。第二部分（第 20-27 页）试图解释在跨国银行业历史的各个阶段，某些群体和某些国家占据主导地位的原因。为应对这一更广泛的问题，有必要将传统理论嵌入一个更通用的框架中，该框架既包含社会和政治视角，也包含经济视角。</span></span></span>

Definitions<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">定义</span></span></span>
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Different types of analysis may require different definitions. Thus the definition of a bank that is most useful for the study of contemporary UK macroeconomic policy may be very different from one that is useful for the business historian. The former, for example, may emphasize credit-creation whilst the latter may instead emphasize the nature of the service supplied to customers. For the business historian, a bank may be defined as an institution specializing in the purchase and sale of currency, or claims to currency. Currency may in turn be defined as a specialized medium of exchange - typically, though not exclusively, bearer notes and minted coins issued on behalf of a government. A multinational bank (MNB) is simply a bank that owns and controls banking activities in two or more countries.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">不同类型的分析可能需要不同的定义。因此，对于研究当代英国宏观经济政策最实用的银行定义，可能与商业历史学家所需的定义大相径庭。前者可能侧重信用创造，而后者则可能更强调向客户提供服务的本质。对于商业历史学家而言，银行可被定义为专门从事货币或货币债权买卖的机构。货币本身则可被定义为一种专门的交换媒介——通常（尽管并非唯一）指代政府发行的不记名票据和铸币。跨国银行 (MNB) 即指在两个或以上国家拥有并控制银行业务的银行。</span></span></span>

There are plenty of opportunities for specialization within banking. Thus banking itself is not a homogeneous activity, but <span id="Ch02.xhtml_page_15"></span>rather a collection of distinct though interdependent activities which can, and often are, carried on by different people, and in different locations. Thus while deposit-taking, lending and currency exchange are often considered 'core' activities, banks also engage in the marketing of securities, note issue, and so on. Some 'universal' banks carry on a wide range of such activities whilst others - such as domestic clearing banks, trade banks and investment banks - are traditionally (though not invariably) more narrowly focused.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">银行业内存在大量的专业化机会。因此，银行业本身并非同质化的活动，而是一系列虽然相互独立却又彼此依存的业务集合——这些业务可以、也常常由不同的人在不同的地点来开展。例如，尽管吸收存款、发放贷款和货币兑换常被视为“核心”业务，银行同样会涉足证券营销、纸币发行等环节。一些“全能型”银行广泛开展此类业务，而另一些银行——如国内清算银行、贸易银行和投资银行——虽然在传统上（并非绝对）业务范围相对更窄。</span></span></span>

Horizontal and vertical integration<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">横向与纵向整合</span></span></span>
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Since so much research has been done on manufacturing multinationals<sup>2</sup> compared to other types, it is natural to approach multinational banking by enquiring how far the behaviour of MNBs parallels that of manufacturing firms. The short answer is that, while the governing economic principles are the same, the way that these principles work out in practice is rather different.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">鉴于对制造业跨国公司的研究远多于其他类型，自然会将跨国银行的研究方向转向探究跨国银行的行为在多大程度上与制造业企业相似。简而言之，尽管主导的经济原理相同，但这些原理在实际运作中的方式却大相径庭。</span></span></span>

There are two main types of manufacturing multinational - the horizontally-integrated firm and the vertically-integrated one. Horizontal integration is very common in manufacturing. The obvious parallel to the horizontally-integrated manufacturer is the multi-domestic MNB, which replicates self-contained domestic banking operations in different countries. This form is very rare, because of a combination of low monopolistic advantages on the one hand, and high barriers to foreign entry on the other, as explained below. Vertical integration is a more potent force in multinational banking although, for reasons that will become apparent, the internalization of vertical linkages actually has the appearance of horizontal integration where banks are concerned.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">制造业跨国公司主要有两种类型：横向一体化企业与纵向一体化企业。横向一体化在制造业中非常普遍。与横向一体化制造商明显相似的是多国国内 MNB（即在不同国家复制独立国内银行业务的机构）。但这种形式极为罕见，原因如下：一方面垄断优势较低，另一方面外国进入壁垒较高。纵向一体化在跨国银行业中是一种更强大的力量，尽管——出于下文将阐明的原因——纵向联系的内部化实际上呈现为横向一体化的形态，就银行而言。</span></span></span>

The location of banking facilities<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">银行设施的选址</span></span></span>
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Location patterns in banking are very different from those in most manufacturing industries, though the reasons for this are fairly subtle. Consider the two simplistic propositions: 'Banks are part of the service sector and services are not readily tradeable; hence banks must be located where the customer resides' and 'Banking services are tradeable because they involve information which can be transmitted by telecommunications and documents which can be sent by post; because tariffs are difficult to levy on transmissions and documents, trade in banking is potentially freer than in most manufacturing industries'.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">银行业的地点分布模式与大多数制造业截然不同，尽管背后的原因相当微妙。考虑以下两种简单化的说法：一是"银行属于服务业，而服务不易交易，因此银行必须设在客户所在地"；二是"银行服务是可交易的，因为它们涉及可通过电信传输的信息和可通过邮寄发送的文件；由于对传输和文件难以征收关税，银行业的贸易潜力比大多数制造业更为自由"。</span></span></span>

Both of these propositions have important elements of truth in <span id="Ch02.xhtml_page_16"></span>them. Individuals do not like carrying large amounts of cash around with them, and it is risky to transmit cash by post. Deposit banks therefore need to locate close to their customers so that the customers can call in on local shopping trips. This insight is captured by the first proposition.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">这两个命题都有其重要的真实成分。人们不喜欢随身携带大量现金，而且通过邮寄现金也存在风险。因此，存款银行需要设在客户附近，以便客户在本地购物时能顺道办理业务。第一个命题就体现了这一见解。</span></span></span>

Bills and longer-term financial claims can be transported more easily and securely than cash, however, and the customers involved - usually importers and exporters - tend to agglomerate around entrepot centres - ports where organized markets and large-scale processing activities emerge. Trade banks can therefore operate with a much lower density of banking facilities, concentrated on centres of this kind.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">然而，汇票及长期金融债权相比现金更容易安全运输，而相关客户（通常为进出口商）往往聚集在转口贸易中心——即依托港口形成的有组织市场和大规模加工活动的枢纽。因此，贸易银行只需在中心区域集中布局少量金融设施即可高效运作。</span></span></span>

Certificates of long-term debt can be easily transported too. Investment banks, which specialize in the marketing of long-term government and corporate debt, cater for a sophisticated clientele of governments, major firms, pension funds, investment trusts and wealthy individuals, many of which have offices in major financial centres, or can be easily reached by salesmen based at these centres. Thus the investment bank can operate a global service through offices located in just a few centres. In this way the location patterns of trade banks and investment banks highlight the significance of the second proposition.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">长期债务凭证同样便于携带。专门从事长期政府及企业债券营销的投资银行，服务于政府、大型企业、养老基金、投资信托及高净值个人等成熟客户群体——这些客户大多在主要金融中心设有办事处，或能轻松联系到驻守这些中心的销售人员。因此，投资银行仅需在少数金融中心设立分支机构，就能开展全球业务。贸易银行与投资银行在区位布局上的差异，恰恰印证了第二个命题的重要性。</span></span></span>

Advances in transport and communications have significantly influenced patterns of bank location, and will continue to do so in the future. At the present, personal visits to a deposit bank are becoming less important with the spread of electronic point-of-sale equipment and the proliferation of cash-dispensers in both traditional shopping centres and new out-of-town centres serving the motorist. At the same time, greater mobility induced by car ownership and jet travel is increasing the need to access deposits when visiting distant locations. This means that information-flows between bank facilities need to be more closely integrated than before. This encourages banks to disperse their facilities over a wider area.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">交通和通信的进步显著影响了银行的选址模式，未来这一趋势仍将持续。如今，随着电子销售点设备的普及以及自动取款机在传统购物中心和新兴郊区汽车服务区的广泛布设，个人亲临存款银行办理业务的需求已逐渐降低。与此同时，汽车拥有率和航空旅行带来的更高流动性，使得人们在前往异地时存取款的需求日益增加。这意味着银行设施之间的信息流通必须比以往更加紧密地整合。这促使银行将服务网点分散布局到更广阔的区域。</span></span></span>

The growth of containerization in freight transport is undermining the status of traditional entrepot centres, in the sense that goods in transit are no longer open to inspection on transhipment and so are difficult to trade at this stage. More deals are arranged in advance, with goods being consigned intact from door to door. The traditional entrepot is therefore being replaced by the international service centre where these advanced deals are arranged.<sup>3</sup> This service centre may be a traditional entrepot with an expanded role, or it may be a new centre altogether. Such changes call for a relocation of the premises of trade banks.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">货运集装箱化的发展正在削弱传统转口中心的地位，因为中转货物在转运时不再接受查验，从而难以在此阶段进行交易。更多交易提前安排，货物直接从门到门整批运输。因此，传统转口中心正被国际服务中心取代，后者负责安排这些提前达成的交易。 <sup>3</sup> 这种服务中心可能是功能扩展后的传统转口中心，也可能是一个全新的中心。此类变化要求贸易银行的营业场所重新布局。</span></span></span>

<span id="Ch02.xhtml_page_17"></span>Advances in communications have strongly affected investment banking, since clients can now easily make contact with banks anywhere in their own time zone (and with banks the world over if they are prepared to do business outside normal office hours). This globalization of the market has facilitated the export of investment banking services, and means that it is only the need for representation in different time zones, and for face-to-face contact to close a deal, that drives banks to meet the need of this market with more than one facility.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">通信技术的进步对投资银行业产生了深远影响，因为客户现在可以轻松地在自己所在时区内的任何银行取得联系（如果他们愿意在正常工作时间之外进行交易，甚至可以联系全球各地的银行）。市场的全球化促进了投资银行服务的出口，这意味着，银行之所以需要设立多个分支机构来满足市场需求，仅仅是为了在不同时区保持存在感，以及为了达成交易所需的面对面接触。</span></span></span>

Monopolistic advantage<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">垄断优势</span></span></span>
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

In manufacturing, monopolistic advantage is typically based on proprietary product or process technology emanating from R & D, Advances are embodied in information which has potential application in world-wide markets and can be transferred, when there are barriers to export, to overseas facilities.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">在制造业中，垄断优势通常基于研发所带来的专属产品或工艺技术。这些进步体现在具有全球市场潜在应用价值的信息中，并且在出口存在障碍时，可以转移至海外设施。</span></span></span>

If the information could be licensed, however, then there would be no need for multinational operation. Obstacles to licensing arise, for example, because information is difficult to patent. Property rights in information must be protected by secrecy, and this makes it difficult to give quality assurance to the potential licensee. There are also difficulties when licensees' market areas potentially overlap, because price collusion through market segmentation - which is necessary for the licensor to appropriate monopoly rents - is then difficult for the licensor to police.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">然而，如果信息可以通过许可方式获得，那么跨国运营就并非必需。许可方式存在障碍，例如信息难以申请专利。信息产权必须通过保密来保护，这导致难以向潜在被许可方提供质量保证。当被许可方的市场区域可能重叠时，也会出现问题，因为通过市场分割实现的价格共谋——这是许可方攫取垄断租金所必需的——此时便难以监控。</span></span></span>

Monopolistic advantages in banking rarely satisfy these conditions. There are, of course, special banking-related skills, such as knowledge of sophisticated accounting practices and experience of evaluating specialist collateral for loans.<sup>4</sup> These advantages, however, are not only difficult to patent, but difficult to keep secret too. The monopoly element is usually missing; the skills can often be acquired simply by hiring a consultant or employee who has had suitable training. In so far as there is monopoly, it may reside purely in the superior judgement of a particular employee.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">银行业中的垄断优势很少能满足这些条件。当然，银行业也存在着一些特殊技能，比如精通复杂的会计实务，以及评估专业贷款抵押品的经验。 <sup>4</sup> 然而，这些优势不仅难以申请专利，而且也难以保密。垄断元素通常缺失，这些技能往往只需雇佣一位受过适当培训的顾问或员工即可获得。如果存在垄断的话，它可能纯粹体现在某位特定员工的卓越判断力上。</span></span></span>

The main source of monopoly rents in an MNB is probably personal contact. Creating an information network, and developing team-specific skills in the synthesis of information from various sources, may allow a firm to preempt a steady stream of arbitrage or speculative opportunities.<sup>5</sup> Although the information can, in principle, be acquired entirely at arm's length, the establishment of wholly-owned outposts may increase both the quantity and the quality of information gathered.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">跨国银行垄断租金的主要来源可能是个人关系。建立信息网络，并培养整合多方信息的团队专属技能，能够让企业抢占稳定的套利或投机机会。 <sup>5</sup> 尽管从原则上讲，这些信息完全可以通过非关联方式获取，但设立全资分支机构可能会提高所收集信息的数量和质量。</span></span></span>

<span id="Ch02.xhtml_page_18"></span>It should also be noted that there are local monopolistic advantages that firms can acquire by obtaining exclusive charters or licences for note issue, handling military or public service payments, tax farming, and so on. These cannot normally lead to multinational!ty since they are usually specific to a particular locality. A general skill in bidding for such privileges (based, perhaps, on a social skill in manipulating the vanity of politicans) may lead to parallel operations of this kind in different countries, however.<sup>6</sup><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">还应注意，企业通过获取独家特许权或执照（例如发行纸币、处理军事或公共服务支付、包税等）可能获得地方垄断优势。这些优势通常局限于特定地区，因此一般无法促成跨国经营。然而，一种竞标此类特权的通用技能（或许基于利用政治家虚荣心的社交技巧）可能会使企业在不同国家开展类似的平行业务。 <sup>6</sup> </span></span></span>

Another form of monopoly obtained through franchise is the authority given to an investment bank to handle an issue of government or corporate debt, or to place an equity issue on the market.<sup>7</sup> When imperfect information segments capital markets (which it often does in practice, although conventional finance theory tends to ignore the fact), a given type of debt issued by a given corporate body may be perceived by some investors as a differentiated product affording a unique set of risk-attributes. This affords the issuer potential monopoly rents, but to appropriate these successfully potential price competition from different sales outlets employed by the investment bank needs to be controlled. Syndication - that is, the licensing of a set of independent banks to place quotas of 'debt' - may fail to prevent such competition, and as a result sales outlets may become wholly-owned. When sales outlets in different countries are brought under common ownership, this creates a multinational investment bank.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">另一种通过特许经营获得的垄断形式，是赋予投资银行处理政府或公司债券发行、或承销股票上市的权限。 <sup>7</sup> 当信息不对称分割资本市场时（尽管传统金融理论常忽视这一事实，但在实际操作中这并不罕见），特定公司发行的某类债券，可能被部分投资者视为具备独特风险特征的差异化产品。这为发行方创造了潜在的垄断租金，但要成功获取这些租金，就必须控制投资银行所采用的不同销售渠道之间可能出现的价格竞争。银团贷款——即授权一组独立银行配售一定额度的“债务”——或许无法阻止这类竞争，因此销售渠道可能转为全资拥有。 当不同国家的销售渠道被纳入统一所有权时，这就催生了一家跨国投资银行。</span></span></span>

When technology transfer draws a firm to a new location abroad, the bank which has serviced the expanding firm at its home location has an advantage over indigenous firms in serving the new foreign subsidiary. This advantage is goodwill derived from special knowledge of the customer's requirements and it is a monopolistic advantage which is not patentable and which can be transferred abroad. The internalization of this advantage transforms the domestic bank into an MNB.<sup>8</sup> The magnitude of the advantage is variable, however; it is reduced if the foreign environment is very different and if the expansion is by acquisition rather than greenfield investment - two characteristics which often go together.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">当技术转移吸引一家公司迁往海外新地点时，曾在本地为其扩张提供服务的银行，在服务这家新的海外子公司时，相比本地银行具有优势。这种优势来源于对客户需求的深入了解所积累的商誉，它是一种无法申请专利、但可向海外转移的垄断性优势。当这种优势被内部化时，本土银行便转型为跨国银行（MNB）。 <sup>8</sup> 不过，这种优势的大小会因情况而异——如果海外环境与本国差异巨大，或者扩张是通过收购而非绿地投资实现的（这两点往往同时出现），那么优势便会减弱。</span></span></span>

A special kind or advantage that has received less attention than it deserves is the cultural advantage that certain institutions enjoy for confidentiality and political neutrality. Switzerland has, at various times, provided a convenient environment for the accumulation of profits from tax evasion, smuggling, transfer pricing and wartime pillage. Like other local advantages, however, the advantage that Swiss banks derive from the political neutrality is <span id="Ch02.xhtml_page_19"></span>not transferable, since customers could not be guaranteed similar privileges in foreign subsidiary locations.<sup>9</sup> This does not, of course, prevent the banks from opening representative offices to promote the export of these services by the parent firm. But multinational operations analogous to those of manufacturing firms are not really feasible.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">一种备受忽视的特殊优势，是某些机构因保密性和政治中立而享有的文化优势。瑞士曾多次为逃税、走私、转让定价及战时掠夺所得利润的积累提供便利环境。然而，与其他本地优势一样，瑞士银行从政治中立中获取的优势并不具可转移性——因为客户在外国分支机构所在地无法获得同等特权保障。 <sup>9</sup> 这当然不妨碍银行设立代表处来推广母公司服务的出口，但开展类似制造业企业的跨国经营实际上并不可行。</span></span></span>

A combination of local advantages in two different countries may generate a special kind of multinationality, however. Privileged access to capital in one location may be combined with local advantages elsewhere to create a 'free-standing' firm, which has an office where the capital is raised - to maintain the firm's reputation - and largely autonomous executive offices directing local operations overseas.<sup>10</sup><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">然而，两个不同国家的本地优势相结合，可能会催生出一种特殊的跨国企业形式。在某地享有资本获取特权的企业，若能与其他地方的本地优势相结合，便可形成一种“独立式”企业——它在筹集资本的地点设立办事处以维系公司声誉，同时拥有高度自主的执行办公室，主导海外业务的运营。 <sup>10</sup> </span></span></span>

Network linkages<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">网络联系</span></span></span>
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The vertically-integrated manufacturing multinational brings upstream and downstream activities under common control. This form of enterprise has a parallel in the multinational trade banks which are commonly found in closely-integrated regional or global networks of trade.<sup>11</sup> Compared to manufacturing, there are two special features of note about this kind of MNB.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">垂直整合的制造业跨国公司将上下游活动纳入统一管控。这类企业形态与跨国贸易银行异曲同工，后者通常活跃于联系紧密的区域性或全球性贸易网络中。 <sup>11</sup> 与制造业相比，此类跨国银行有两大特殊之处值得关注。</span></span></span>

The first is that the flow between facilities is usually two-way, with bills issued at each location being discounted at the other. The second is that several different locations - typically regional entrepôts - are involved in the network, creating a complex set of pairwise linkages. Multilateral trade within such networks is frequently rationalized by one centre emerging as a hub. Bills issued at this hub can be used to settle transactions between locations around the rim. The logical way for the bank to organize its trade is to headquarter its operation at the hub and own a comprehensive set of facilities covering principal locations around the rim.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">首先，各设施之间的资金流动通常是双向的，每个地点开出的汇票可在另一地点贴现。其次，网络中往往涉及多个不同地点——通常是区域性转口港——从而形成一系列复杂的配对关联。此类网络内的多边贸易常常通过某一中心枢纽的崛起而得以合理化。在这一枢纽开出的汇票，可用于结算周边各地点之间的交易。银行组织贸易的逻辑做法是：将业务总部设在枢纽，并在周边主要地点拥有全面的设施布局。</span></span></span>

A trade bank can, of course, operate entirely through correspondent relations with other banks and not own any overseas facilities at all. If there is some doubt about the integrity of a correspondent, however, then internalization of the linkage through the establishment of an overseas activity may be preferred.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">当然，贸易银行完全可以完全通过与其他银行的代理行关系来运作，而无需拥有任何海外设施。然而，如果对代理行的诚信存有疑虑，那么通过设立海外业务来实现联系内部化，可能是更优的选择。</span></span></span>

On the other hand, the costs of this may be high unless the volume of business is already large, and if the volume is large then the correspondent may take revenge by establishing its own overseas affiliate in the investor's home ground. Theory predicts, therefore, that foreign ownership will develop for the finance of trade either in newly-opened territories, where reliable <span id="Ch02.xhtml_page_20"></span>correspondents are difficult to find, or in well-established territories with which there is a very large volume of trade. This large volume of trade may be based on certain staple commodities - cotton, jute, silk, copper, and so on - in the valuing of which the bank attains considerable experience. Links with well-established territories whose trade is dominated by linkages with other locations will tend to be handled by correspondent arrangements.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">另一方面，除非业务量已经很大，否则这样做成本可能很高；而如果业务量很大，代理行可能会通过在投资者本国设立自己的海外分支机构来进行报复。因此，理论预测，外国所有权将出现在以下两种情况下的贸易融资中：要么是新开辟的地区，难以找到可靠的代理行；要么是已建立成熟贸易关系且贸易量极大的地区。这种巨大的贸易量可能基于某些大宗商品——棉花、黄麻、丝绸、铜等——银行在评估这些商品价值时积累了丰富的经验。对于与那些贸易受到其他地区关联主导的成熟地区之间的联系，往往通过代理行安排来处理。</span></span></span>

It should be noted that while the logic of the linkages of the trade-oriented MNB is that of two-way vertical integration, the actual structure that emerges is one of horizontal integration. This is because the two-way flows along the linkages oblige the facilities to replicate each other in both purchasing and presenting bills.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">需要注意的是，虽然以贸易为导向的跨国银行（MNB）的关联逻辑是双向垂直整合，但实际形成的结构却是水平整合。这是因为沿着这些关联渠道的双向流动，迫使相关设施在采购和账单呈现上相互复制。</span></span></span>

Contractual arrangements: some social and political considerations<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">契约安排：一些社会与政治考量</span></span></span>
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

To understand a theory at a really deep level it is often useful to embed that theory into a still wider framework. It has been argued elsewhere that it is useful to embed the theory of multinationals within a general theory of contractual arrangements over space.<sup>12</sup> This more general approach is particularly useful to the historian because it encompasses the enormous diversity of institutional forms that are found in different places at different periods. It emphasizes that the kind of contractual arrangement required for co-ordination over space depends upon the level of trust between the parties involved. High trust, whether promoted by cultural ambience or family ties, allows sophisticated co-ordination under even quite primitive legal arrangements.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">要想从更深层次理解一个理论，将其嵌入一个更广阔的框架中往往很有裨益。曾有观点指出，将跨国公司理论纳入一个关于空间上契约安排的普遍理论中，是很有用的。 <sup>12</sup> 这种更全面的方法对历史学家尤其有帮助，因为它涵盖了不同时期、不同地点所出现的多种多样的制度形式。它强调，在空间上实现协调所需的契约安排，取决于相关各方之间的信任程度。无论是通过文化氛围还是家族纽带所促成的高信任度，即使在相当原始的法治环境下，也能实现复杂的协调。</span></span></span>

Some of the more pertinent aspects of this broader approach are summarised below:<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">以下总结了这一更广阔视角中一些更为相关的方面：</span></span></span>

1.  If two economic agents are in such agreement that they think as one, then they have no need to submit themselves to common control. Thus two parties, one in each country, may be able to co-ordinate their activities without either arm's length transaction or submission to common control. More generally, two people who trust each other's integrity and judgement may be able to co-ordinate simply by sharing information freely and allowing each other to act as they think best. Successful co-ordination will give the appearance of organization to the outsider even though inside there is no line of authority present. The ability of the early Rothschild bankers to co-ordinate their Paris and Frankfurt operations may be an instance of this.<sup>13</sup><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
    <span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">倘若两个经济主体思想高度一致、同心同德，便无需屈从于统一管控。因此，分属两国的当事方，既不必保持距离交易，也无需受制于共同控制，就能协调行动。更广泛地说，两个相互信任对方操守与判断力的人，只需自由分享信息，并放手让对方自行决策，便能达成默契。这种成功的协调在外人看来如同精心组织的运作，实则内部并无任何权力层级。早期罗斯柴尔德银行家族能够协调巴黎与法兰克福的业务，或许就是明证。 <sup>13</sup> </span></span></span>
2.  <span id="Ch02.xhtml_page_21"></span>In so-called primitive economic systems, centralized control is often effected through paternal authority. So long as sons consent to subordination to their father, for example, family organization can extend multinationally by sending junior family members abroad to take charge of local facilities. There is no need for formal links in the sense of a parental majority equity stake. Cultures which emphasize ancestor-worship and respect for the aged may be able to rely heavily upon this mechanism. This may account for some of the subtle international co-ordination effected by the overseas Chinese.<sup>14</sup> Paternalism was also a key element in Victorian values, but has little place in contemporary western cultures. The decline of paternalism may be linked to the decline of the large family firm in western industrial economies, but this can be no more than speculation at this stage.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
    <span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">在所谓的原始经济体系中，中央集权往往通过父权权威来实现。例如，只要儿子们同意服从父亲，家族组织就可以通过派遣年幼的家族成员前往国外管理当地设施，从而实现跨国扩张。无需像母公司持有多数股权那样建立正式联系。强调祖先崇拜和尊重长辈的文化可能能够高度依赖这一机制。这或许可以解释海外华人如何实现某些微妙的国际协调。 <sup>14</sup> 父权主义也是维多利亚时代价值观的关键要素，但在当代西方文化中几乎没有立足之地。父权主义的衰落可能与西方工业经济中大型家族企业的衰落有关，但这在现阶段只能是推测。</span></span></span>
3.  Historically the growth of private property and the growth of individualism, it has been claimed, are closely linked.<sup>15</sup> It is important to recognize however, that contracts involving private property may be used to create arrangements in which group-oriented behaviour has an important role. The persistence of the partnership structure in banking long after the joint-stock form was introduced shows that co-operation between trusted partners could be sustained without a more formal system of monitoring. The continuing use of 50:50 equity joint venture, in which there is a formal ambiguity of control, shows that when people trust each other, it is group loyalty and not legal sanction that underpins co-ordination.<sup>16</sup><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
    <span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">历史上，私有财产的增长与个人主义的发展被认为紧密相连。 <sup>15</sup> 然而，重要的是要认识到，涉及私有财产的契约也可能被用来构建以集体导向行为为核心的安排。在股份制引入后，银行业仍长期保留合伙制结构，这证明了在缺乏正式监督体系的情况下，受信任的合作伙伴之间仍能维持协作。而 50:50 股权合资企业的持续存在，其中控制权存在模糊性，表明当人们彼此信任时，维系协调的是群体忠诚而非法律制裁。 <sup>16</sup> </span></span></span>
4.  A key step in the evolution of the private property system was the emergence of tradeable debt - that is, long-term claims which could be resold by the creditor. The securitization of the national debt, which took off around the turn of the eighteenth century, widened the market for government loans by increasing their liquidity.<sup>17</sup> It stimulated banking by providing a safe source of investment of deposits, and created opportunities for underwriting issues for sale to the public. The securitization of private debt which occurred about the same time was associated with the rise of joint stock companies, and created a potential market in corporate control. The existence of a large proportion of shares which were actively traded on a stock exchange created a takeover threat which disciplined management to follow market sentiments. It became much more difficult to perpetuate the high-trust ethos of the family firm in the presence of this discipline. Hierarchical control of management, based on a board of directors overseeing a chief <span id="Ch02.xhtml_page_22"></span>executive who controlled a pyramid of subordinate specialists, became the system through which market discipline was passed down the line. Many banks were initially insulated from such discipline, however, because they confined share transactions to family members, or to firms in related lines of business (shipping, insurance, and so on) which provided custom for, or bartered information with, the firm. To some extent government regulations prohibiting or discouraging foreign takeovers have continued to insulate joint stock banks from the full rigour of competition in the market for corporate control.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
    <span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">私有财产制度演进中的一个关键步骤，是可交易债务的出现——即债权人能够转售的长期债权。大约在 18 世纪之交兴起的国债证券化，通过提高流动性扩大了政府贷款的融资市场。 <sup>17</sup> 它为存款投资提供了安全渠道，刺激了银行业发展，并创造了向公众承销发行机会。几乎同时发生的私人债务证券化，与股份公司的兴起相伴而生，并催生了公司控制权的潜在市场。大量股份在证券交易所活跃交易，形成了接管威胁，迫使管理层遵循市场情绪。在这种约束下，家族企业那种高度信任的文化传统变得难以维系。 基于董事会对首席执行官进行监督，而首席执行官又控制着一个由下级专家组成的金字塔式层级结构，这种分层管理模式成为市场纪律层层传递的制度。然而，许多银行最初并未受到这种纪律的约束，因为它们将股票交易限制在家族成员或相关业务领域（如航运、保险等）的企业之间——这些企业要么为银行提供客户资源，要么与之交换信息。在某种程度上，政府禁止或限制外资收购的法规，仍使股份制银行得以免受公司控制权市场全面竞争的冲击。</span></span></span>
5.  Although a few studies of multinationals have considered state-owned firms, there has been little recognition that the imperial state is itself a kind of multinational enterprise, operating defence, police and judicial activities in several different countries. In some cases the state itself can act as an MNB - the Roman Treasury, for example, co-ordinated the minting of coins in different colonies.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
    <span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">尽管关于跨国公司的研究中有少数涉及国有企业，但很少有人意识到，帝国国家本身就是一种跨国企业，在多个不同国家运营着国防、警察和司法活动。在某些情况下，国家本身也可以扮演 MNB 的角色——例如，罗马国库曾协调不同殖民地的铸币事务。</span></span></span>

Imperialism is a form of international political integration in which one country subordinates others, but there are other forms of political integration in which there is, at least in theory, greater equality. The second half of the nineteenth century was notable not only for imperialism but also for national unification, notably in Germany and Italy. Conversely the decline of imperialism in the twentieth century was associated with national disintegration such as the break-up of the Austro-Hungarian state.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">帝国主义是一种国际政治整合形式，其中一国支配其他国；但还有其他形式的政治整合，至少在理论上更为平等。19 世纪后半叶不仅以帝国主义著称，也见证了民族统一，尤其是在德国和意大利。相反，20 世纪帝国主义的衰落则伴随着民族分裂，如奥匈帝国的解体。</span></span></span>

The formation of the nation state through political unification parallels, in an instructive way, the formation of a multinational enterprise through a merger of local firms. The economic gains from unification - economies from the rationalization of defence, greater bargaining power against outsiders, and so on - parallel some of the gains available to the multinational firm. Although such benefits could, in theory, be achieved through arm's-length negotiations between independent states, it is administratively simpler to internalize the process by making a single comprehensive treaty of unification.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">通过政治统一形成民族国家的过程，与地方企业合并形成跨国公司的过程具有启发性地相似。统一带来的经济收益——例如国防合理化带来的节约、对外谈判时更强的议价能力等——与跨国企业所能获得的某些收益相类似。尽管理论上这些利益可以通过独立国家之间的公平协商实现，但通过制定单一的综合统一条约将这一过程内部化，在行政上更为简便。</span></span></span>

Political unification and imperial disintegration also have implications for the question of whether a firm is multinational or not. A multi-principality German enterprise prior to German unification becomes a uninational German enterprise afterwards. Conversely, uninational Vienna-based enterprises became multinationals overnight following the break-up of the Austro-Hungarian state. In cases where the process of disintegration is extremely <span id="Ch02.xhtml_page_23"></span>protracted (such as the Ottoman Empire in the Balkans and in Cyprus), it may be difficult to determine where an enterprise is truly multinational or not.<sup>18</sup><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">政治统一与帝国解体对企业是否属于跨国企业这一问题也有着深刻影响。在德国统一之前，一家跨越多公国的德国企业，在统一后便成了纯粹的德国本土企业。反之，原本总部设在维也纳的单一国家企业，在奥匈帝国解体后，一夜之间就变成了跨国企业。而当解体过程极其漫长时（例如奥斯曼帝国在巴尔干和塞浦路斯的情况），就难以判断某家企业究竟是否真正属于跨国性质了。 <sup>18</sup> </span></span></span>

Learning processes and the growth of the bank<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">学习过程与银行的成长</span></span></span>
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The ability of firms to internalize the feedback from previous experience is a major factor governing their growth. In family firms this feedback can often become lost when the new generation fails to enter the business with adequate practical experience and tutoring from the older generation. The more formal promotion procedures of the managerially-controlled firm offer one way out of this difficulty, though with the potential risk of delaying promotion until youthful ambition and imagination have disappeared altogether.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">企业吸收过往经验反馈的能力，是其增长的关键因素。在家族企业中，若新一代缺乏足够的实践经验且未得到老一辈的悉心指导，这种反馈往往容易流失。经理控制型企业更为正规的晋升机制为这一困境提供了出路，但潜在风险在于，晋升可能被推迟，直至年轻一代的雄心与创造力荡然无存。</span></span></span>

One factor encouraging the new generation of a family firm to remain within the business is that the family belongs to an ethnic or religious minority which finds other avenues of advancement denied it. An important category of minority is the immigrant refugee. Refugees are often the victims of expulsion because of their commercial success in their previous country of residence, and so bring with them established business skills. They also have a detailed knowledge of potential trading operations with their former country. This combination of business skill, international knowledge and exclusion from advancement except by business means can provide a powerful factor not only in building but, most importantly, in sustaining multinational operations. The Jews, the Huguenots, the Armenians and the overseas Indians and Chinese all fall into this category to some extent.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">促使家族企业新一代成员继续留在企业中的因素之一是，该家族属于某个民族或宗教少数群体，而这类群体在其他晋升途径上往往受限。难民移民便是一个重要的少数群体类别。他们往往因在原籍国取得商业成功而被迫流亡，因此带来了成熟的商业技能。同时，他们对与原籍国之间潜在贸易往来了如指掌。这种商业技能、国际视野以及商业之外晋升渠道被堵死的处境，不仅为建立、更重要的是为维持跨国经营提供了强大动力。犹太人、胡格诺派教徒、亚美尼亚人，以及海外印度人和华人在某种程度上均属此类。</span></span></span>

Some family firms, particularly those founded by oppressed minorities, may have rather disreputable beginnings, with the initial fund of investable resources being accumulated by 'black economy' tax-evasion or quite sinister illegal activities.<sup>19</sup> Subsequently the family may become respectable, and a corporate myth emerge to glorify the founder and legitimate his work. The danger, from the point of view of business performance, is that respectability may be pushed too far, and the level of entrepreneurship (which is often geared to being unconventional) be reduced as a consequence.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">有些家族企业，尤其是那些由受压迫的少数族裔创立的，可能有着不太光彩的起步，最初的可用投资资金是通过"黑色经济"逃税或相当阴险的非法活动积累起来的。 <sup>19</sup> 后来，这个家族可能会变得体面，并出现一种企业神话来美化创始人，使其事业合法化。从商业表现的角度来看，风险在于体面可能被推向极致，进而导致企业家精神（往往倾向于打破常规）的水平下降。</span></span></span>

Applying the theory<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">理论应用</span></span></span>
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Economic theory is sometimes dismissed as being static and hence unsuited to historical explanation. This is an overstatement. Even <span id="Ch02.xhtml_page_24"></span>static economics can explain, using the comparative method, how behaviour changes when the environment changes. Economic theory also facilitates dynamic analysis, as the discussion in the previous section shows.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">经济理论有时被斥为静态的，因此不适合用于历史解释。这种说法有些夸大其词。即使是静态经济学，也能通过比较方法解释行为在环境变化时如何改变。正如上一节的讨论所示，经济理论也促进了动态分析。</span></span></span>

Perhaps the most important point to recognize is that economics does not make the exaggerated claims that are sometimes imputed to it. Most economic theory is couched in terms of the representative firm - an 'ideal type' - rather than an actual firm. This ideal type represents the average firm in a given population. It abstracts from the many idiosyncracies of any particular firm. It is presumed that the idiosyncracies have a random rather than a systematic aspect to them - if they were systematic they would be introduced into the picture of the representative firm instead. The central limit theorem implies that under certain conditions these idiosyncratic effects will cancel out so far as average behaviour in the industry is concerned. Thus while economics claims to identify measurable statistical effects in a population of firms, it does not claim to explain everything about each firm in the population. The relevance of economics cannot be rejected by a single anecdote which describes some bizarre behaviour by an individual entrepreneur. It can only be rejected by showing that the systematic effects it predicts are not significant once random elements have been filtered out. Economic theory survives because in most recorded instances the systematic effects turn out to be significant; indeed economic theory generally passes this test with a considerable margin to spare.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">或许最需要认清的一点是，经济学并未做出某些强加于它的夸大其词。大多数经济理论都是以代表型企业——一种“理想类型”——而非真实企业为对象展开论述的。这种理想类型代表了特定群体中的平均企业，它抽象掉了任何特定企业的诸多独特性。这些独特性被假定具有随机性而非系统性——倘若它们具有系统性，就会被纳入代表型企业的图景之中。中心极限定理表明，在一定条件下，这些独特性效应在行业平均行为层面会相互抵消。因此，经济学虽然声称能够识别出企业群体中可衡量的统计效应，但并不声称能解释该群体中每个企业的所有方面。 经济学的意义，并不会因为某个企业家的奇特行为而被否定。唯有在剔除随机因素后，其预测的系统性效应不再显著时，才能被推翻。经济学理论之所以屹立不倒，是因为在大多数有据可查的案例中，这些系统性效应都表现得相当显著；事实上，经济学理论通常能以相当宽裕的余地通过这一检验。</span></span></span>

To illustrate the application of comparative static methods we concentrate here on two particularly crucial episodes in multinational banking history. The first involves the application of conventional internalization theory and concerns the dramatic post-war expansion of US retail banking into Europe and elsewhere. The second involves the application of the broader socioeconomic approach and concerns the replacement of the family partnership by the joint stock company as the dominant form of MNB in the second half of the nineteenth century.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">为了说明比较静态法的应用，我们聚焦于跨国银行史上两个尤为关键的阶段。第一个阶段涉及传统内部化理论的应用，聚焦于二战后美国零售银行业向欧洲及其他地区迅猛扩张的浪潮。第二个阶段则涉及更广泛的社会经济视角，探讨 19 世纪下半叶家族合伙制如何被股份公司取代，成为跨国银行的主导组织形式。</span></span></span>

The first case is relatively straightforward. The growth of foreign direct investment in manufacturing by high-technology mass-marketed-oriented firms created a new demand for corporate banking services overseas; corporate clients wished to deal with English-speaking bankers familiar with US as well as with indigenous banking methods. These requirements could have been met in several ways:<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">第一种情况相对直观。高科技、面向大众市场的制造业企业对外直接投资增长，催生了海外企业银行服务的新需求——这些企业客户希望与熟悉美国及本土银行操作方式的英语银行家打交道。满足这些需求本有几种途径：</span></span></span>

1.  by the export of banking services from the US;<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
    <span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">从美国直接出口银行服务；</span></span></span>
2.  <span id="Ch02.xhtml_page_25"></span>by correspondent relations between US and overseas banks; and<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
    <span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">通过美国银行与海外银行之间的代理行关系；以及</span></span></span>
3.  by the establishment of US subsidiaries overseas.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
    <span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">通过美国银行在海外设立分支机构。</span></span></span>

The objection to (a) is that many banking services require regular face-to-face contact; in any case, cash deposits and withdrawals cannot easily be handled at a distance. The objection to (b) is that the correspondent bank may be inexperienced in meeting US customers' needs, and the US bank cannot easily control the quality of services the correspondent provides. Furthermore, the US bank may benefit very little from goodwill enjoyed by the correspondent bank; the correspondent may be unable to reciprocate by introducing customers to the US bank because there is little foreign direct investment in that direction. Finally, there is the risk that the US bank may inadvertently build up the correspondent into a competitor which can, in the long run, enter the US market. Under these conditions, (c) is the obvious strategy. Thus US banks capitalize on their goodwill by following their customers overseas; the multinationalization of manufacturing firms creates a derived demand for the multinationalization of banks as well.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">反对方案（a）的理由是，许多银行服务需要定期面对面接触；无论如何，现金存取款很难远程处理。反对方案（b）的理由是，代理行可能缺乏满足美国客户需求的经验，且美国银行难以有效控制代理行提供的服务质量。此外，美国银行从代理行积累的商誉中获益甚微；由于该方向的外国直接投资很少，代理行可能无法通过向美国银行介绍客户来回报。最后，还存在美国银行可能不经意间将代理行培养成竞争对手的风险，从长远来看，对方可能进入美国市场。在此情况下，方案（c）显然是明智的选择。 因此，美国银行凭借自身的商誉，跟随客户走向海外；制造业的跨国化也催生了对银行跨国化的衍生需求。</span></span></span>

The second case is more subtle. It was a combination of several contemporary environmental changes which induced the switch to joint stock operations amongst nineteenth-century multinational banks:<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">第二种情况则更为微妙。十九世纪跨国银行转向股份经营，是当时多重环境变迁共同作用的结果：</span></span></span>

1.  national unification in Europe, which reduced the number of small principalities which needed to hire banking services from private banks, and increased the size of public debt issues that banks had to underwrite;<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
    <span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">欧洲各国的统一，使得需要向私人银行雇佣金融服务的小公国数量减少，同时银行需承销的公共债务规模却相应扩大；</span></span></span>
2.  the return from new colonial territories of merchant adventurers and others, with news of demands for large-scale banking activities - financing mineral exports, underwriting loans for infrastructure development, and so on - that indigenous banks were too small and too amateurish to handle;<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
    <span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">来自新殖民地的商业冒险家及其他人士带回信息，表明当地存在对大规模银行业务的需求——如为矿产出口融资、为基础设施发展承销贷款等——而本土银行规模太小、专业性不足，难以应对这些需求。</span></span></span>
3.  the concentration in London, Paris and Berlin of large loanable funds accumulated both by wealthy individuals and financial trusts, looking for a high rate of return through portfolio diversification across a range of speculative investments. This was coupled with rapid growth of a secondary market through new share listings on the stock exchanges; and<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
    <span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">伦敦、巴黎和柏林聚集了大量由富裕个人和金融信托积累的可贷资金，这些资金通过投资组合分散在各类投机项目中以追求高回报率。与此同时，股票交易所的新股上市推动二级市场迅速扩张；</span></span></span>
4.  the growing organizational skills and naval and military strength of the imperial governments, which enabled them to offer protection at a distance for overseas facilities owned by firms headquartered in the home country, and the ability of <span id="Ch02.xhtml_page_26"></span>large firms - particularly those headquartered in the capital to lobby for such protection.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
    <span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">帝国政府的组织能力、海军及军事力量不断增强，使其能够为总部设在本国的公司的海外设施提供远程保护，而大型企业——尤其是总部设在首都的企业——也有能力游说政府提供此类保护。</span></span></span>

The growing size of investment projects meant that family-accumulated funds were no longer sufficient unless very large risks were to be taken, and that outsiders' funds must be tapped instead. Outsiders would not collectively supply adequate funds without acquiring voting shares, and this would result in the dilution of family control. Family banks and partnerships which overextended themselves failed in a quite dramatic way.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">投资项目规模的不断扩大意味着，家族积累的资金已不足以支撑，除非愿意承担极大的风险，因此必须引入外部资金。外部投资者不可能在不获得有投票权股份的情况下集体提供足够的资金，而这将导致家族控制权的稀释。那些过度扩张的家族银行和合伙企业，都以相当戏剧性的方式宣告失败。</span></span></span>

The answer was to agglomerate many small capital injections under a board composed of individuals who held the confidence of investors. The board's reputation served to underwrite the judgement of colonial entrepreneurs about the opportunities for banking investments overseas.<sup>20</sup> The mechanism is very much akin to that which financed railways, ports, plantations, ranches and mines at the same time - indeed, whilst railways and ports formed the physical infrastructure of the new territories, the overseas banks were part of the 'invisible infrastructure' of credit which complemented it.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">答案是，在由一群赢得投资者信任的个人组成的董事会下，将众多小额注资聚合起来。董事会的声誉相当于为殖民地企业家关于海外银行投资机会的判断提供了背书。 <sup>20</sup> 这一机制与同期为铁路、港口、种植园、牧场和矿山融资的方式非常相似——事实上，铁路和港口构成了新领土的有形基础设施，而海外银行则是构成其补充的信贷“无形基础设施”的一部分。</span></span></span>

After flotation of the share issue, the board's main function was to monitor the chief executive overseas. Because the trust on which family members could work was missing in this case, formal reporting procedures were implemented. Because the chief executive, too, was managing a large operation, he would also rely on more formal procedures. Recruitment would be widened beyond family members, allowing greater expertise to be brought to bear on functionally-specialized roles. The managerial hierarchy would thus replace the family partnership. Of course, in some countries, such as Germany, where the family bank was not so well established and the influence of the state was greater, the growth of international banking involved greater reliance on the managerial hierarchy from the outset.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">股票发行后，董事会的主要职责转为监督海外首席执行官。由于该案例中缺乏能让家族成员共同协作的信任基础，正式的报告程序得以实施。同时，由于首席执行官也在管理庞大的业务体系，他同样会依赖更正式的程序。招聘范围将扩大到家族以外的人员，从而让更多专业人才担任职能专精的岗位。如此一来，管理层级将取代家族合伙制。当然，在德国等一些国家，家族银行尚未发展成熟，政府影响力更大，因此国际银行业务的发展从一开始就更加依赖管理层级制。</span></span></span>

In the case of British overseas banks, the continuing function of the board was much less important than its initial one. The main reasons for maintaining the London board were to act as a nexus for links to correspondent banks (which also had offices in London) and to help raise additional funds as required. In the latter context, the role of London as an international financial centre, with its cheap rediscounting facilities, and well-developed equity market, was crucial.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">对于英国海外银行而言，董事会的持续运作远不如其初始设立时那般重要。维持伦敦董事会的主要目的在于，充当与代理行（这些银行在伦敦也设有办事处）联系的纽带，并协助在必要时筹集更多资金。就后者而言，伦敦作为国际金融中心，凭借其低廉的再贴现便利和发达的股票市场，发挥着至关重要的作用。</span></span></span>

The subsequent indigenization of some of these British overseas banks should not necessarily be construed as a failure. It may simply reflect the fact that as the imperialist role of the European <span id="Ch02.xhtml_page_27"></span>powers declined, and their sources of speculative capital were reduced by successive world wars, the importance of the home board as a nexus of correspondent relations and an avenue to the capital market declined. The main problem may be that local buyouts did not occur earlier. For in their anxiety to appear to be doing something (when they should really have been doing nothing), the boards may have interfered counterproductively in matters of detail overseas that they knew little about.<sup>21</sup><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">这些英国海外银行随后被本土化，不应被简单视为失败。这可能仅仅反映了这样一个事实：随着欧洲列强的帝国主义角色逐渐衰落，以及连续的世界大战削减了其投机资本来源，本土董事会作为代理行关系纽带和资本市场通道的重要性也在下降。真正的问题或许在于当地收购未能更早发生。因为当董事会本应袖手旁观时，他们却急于显得有所作为，反而对自身知之甚少的海外事务细节进行了适得其反的干预。 <sup>21</sup> </span></span></span>

Summary and conclusion<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">总结与结论</span></span></span>
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The economic theory of the MNB is a special case of a general approach to economic theory in which the emphasis is on the selection, through competition and rational evaluation of alternatives, of the most efficient organizational form for co-ordinating a division of labour between related activities. This approach, when applied to banking, helps to identify a small number of possible motives for multinational banking which are analogous to similar motives operating in manufacturing, but which manifest themselves differently because of the importance in banking of local rather than global monopolistic advantages and two-way rather than one-way network flows.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">MNB 的经济理论是一般经济理论方法的一个特例，该方法强调通过竞争和对替代方案的理性评估，选择最有效的组织形式来协调相关活动之间的劳动分工。当这种方法应用于银行业时，有助于识别跨国银行可能存在的少数动机，这些动机与制造业中的类似动机相似，但由于银行业中本地垄断优势而非全球垄断优势、以及双向网络流而非单向网络流的重要性，它们的表现形式有所不同。</span></span></span>

The approach is ideally suited to analysing the choice between correspondent arrangements and the operation of a wholly-owned foreign subsidiary. It can also tackle more complex issues, such as the evolution from interlocking family partnerships to joint stock organization. The theory is still, to some degree, in its infancy, and it is to be hoped that theorists will, in the future, be able to respond constructively to theoretical shortcomings revealed by historical work. These shortcomings can, however, be revealed only by a serious attempt to test the theory using the comparative method. The theory, therefore, is an important tool for the banking historian, and one that has important implications for the way in which banking history should be written.<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-wrapper" lang="zh-CN">\
<span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper-theme-none immersive-translate-target-translation-block-wrapper" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1"><span class="notranslate immersive-translate-target-inner immersive-translate-target-translation-theme-none-inner" immersive-translate-translation-element-mark="1">该方法非常适合分析代理行安排与全资外国子公司运营之间的选择问题。它还能处理更复杂的议题，例如从交叉持股的家族合伙制向股份公司的演变。在某种程度上，这一理论仍处于发展初期，希望未来理论家们能够对历史研究揭示的理论缺陷做出建设性回应。然而，这些缺陷只有通过认真尝试运用比较方法检验该理论才能显现。因此，该理论是银行史学者的重要工具，并且对如何书写银行史具有重要启示。</span></span></span>
